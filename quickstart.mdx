Create your first video (Velo) in just minutes. This quickstart guide shows you how to create your first velo in minutes. By the end, you’ll have a polished video ready to share with your team or customers.

### **1. Choose Your Input**

From your **Home** dashboard, select **Generate a Velo**. You have two clear starting points:

- **Paste a Link:** Best for live websites or web apps where you want the browser to move for you.
- **Import File:** Ideal for static content like PDFs, slides, or documents.

### **2. Guide Velo (Assisted Recording)**

Steps for Perfect **Assisted Recording**.

- **Walk us through it:** Click through your product flow and explain what is happening as if talking to a colleague.
- **Natural Language:** Speak naturally; Velo captures your voice to understand the context.
- **Restart if needed:** If you stumble, simply hit **Restart** to try that specific section again without starting the whole project over.

### **3. Choose Your VeloTwin**

Decide who will represent you on camera.

- **My VeloTwin:** Select your personal digital face and voice clone.
- **Public Avatars:** Choose from a library of public twins if you haven't calibrated your own yet.

### **4. Review the Script**

Velo uses mouse movements, microphone input and additional prompts to draft a script.

- **Scene Cards:** Your walkthrough is broken down into small, manageable scenes. Each card shows the scene number, the exact timestamp (for example, 00:00–00:15), the visual action, and the script.
- **Direct Editing:** You can start typing right in the narration box whenever you want to make a change. There’s no need to hunt for an "Edit" button, the boxes are already open and ready for your updates.  If you don't like your changes, just hit the **Reset** button to go back to the original AI-generated script instantly.

### **5. Synthesis and Sharing**

- One-Shot Synthesis: When you click, Make my Velo, our browser agent visits your target link and re-performs every action exactly as you’ve laid it out in your script. It captures a recording of your product alongside your VeloTwin for a perfect, studio-quality finish.

**The Velo Link:** Once complete, share your video via a **Velo Link**
