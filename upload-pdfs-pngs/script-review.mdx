---
title: "Script Review"
description: "Review and edit the AI-generated script scene by scene before the agent records your demo."
---

After Velo generates the script, you land on the **Script Review** page. This is your opportunity to read through what the AI wrote and make edits before the agent goes and records the actual demo. Unlike other creation methods where script editing happens inside the editor after generation, here the script review happens **before** the agent runs — so it's worth taking a moment to get it right.

## How the script is structured

The script is broken into **scenes**. Each scene corresponds to a portion of your demo and includes two parts:

- **Visual** — describes what the agent will focus on or display during that scene (e.g., which file or part of the screen)
- **Narration** — the voiceover text the VeloTwin will speak during that scene

## Navigating scenes

Use the **← →** arrow buttons at the bottom of the page to move between scenes. The current scene number and total scene count are shown (e.g., **SCENE 2 of 6**) along with a timestamp indicating roughly when that scene appears in the demo.

## Editing narration

The **Narration** field for each scene is editable — click directly in the text to make changes. If you want to undo your edits and go back to the AI's original text, click **Reset** next to that scene's narration.

## Giving feedback

Each scene has a **thumbs up / thumbs down** control. Use this to signal to Velo whether the script for that scene is on the right track. This feedback helps improve the quality of regenerated scripts if you choose to regenerate.

## Regenerating the script

If the script needs a more substantial rework, click **Regenerate Script** at the bottom of the page. Velo will rewrite the entire script from your narration transcript. You can regenerate as many times as needed.

<Note>
  The Visual direction shown for each scene reflects what the agent will do — it is not directly editable. If the agent focuses on the wrong part of your content, try regenerating the script or adjusting your additional instructions in VeloTwin Setup.
</Note>

## Running the agent

Once the script reads the way you want it, review the **estimated cost** shown at the bottom of the page — this tells you how many Velo Minutes the demo will consume. When you're happy, click **Go, Make my Velo →**.

Velo will launch the AI agent to record your demo.
