---
title: "Uploading Files"
description: "Add your PDFs and images to start building your Velo demo."
---

After clicking the **Upload PDFs & PNGs** card, you'll land on the file upload screen.

## Adding your files

You can add files in two ways:

- **Drag and drop** - drag your files directly onto the upload area
- **Click to browse** - click the upload area to open your file picker and select files

You can upload multiple files at once. Velo accepts PDF, PNG, JPG, GIF, and WebP files - up to **10 files** and **100 MB per file**.

Once added, your files appear as a list in the upload panel. You can remove any file by clicking the **×** next to it before continuing.

## File order

The order you upload your files is the order Velo will present them during the narration session. If you're uploading a multi-page PDF, the pages will appear in sequence automatically. If you're uploading individual images, arrange them in the order that matches your intended demo flow before uploading.

<Tip>
  If you need to reorder files after uploading, remove them and re-add them in the correct order.
</Tip>

## Continuing to narration

Once your files are uploaded and in the right order, click **Continue**. Velo will open the **Assisted Recording** modal to prepare your narration session.
