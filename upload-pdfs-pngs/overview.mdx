---

Upload PDFs and PNGs lets you build a Velo demo from static files — pitch decks, product screenshots, design mockups, onboarding slides, or any images that show your product. Instead of recording a live session, you upload your files and Velo's AI agent walks through them, narrates each scene, and produces a polished demo.

<Note>
  This method is currently in **Beta**.
</Note>

## When to use it

Upload PDFs and PNGs is the best choice when:

- You have a **pitch deck, slide deck, or set of product screenshots** you want to turn into a demo
- Your product isn't publicly accessible but you have **design files or mockups** to show
- You want to create a demo **without doing a live recording**
- You need to demo a **future feature** that isn't built yet — just mock it up and upload

## What you'll need

- A **Velo account** with the **Chrome extension installed** (required for the Assisted Recording step)
- Files in one of the supported formats: **PDF, PNG, JPG, GIF, or WebP**
- Up to **10 files**, each under **100 MB**

## The flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your files">
    Name your project and upload your PDFs or images.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Walk through your files with narration">
    Velo opens your files in a canvas tab. Walk through them while talking — your voice guides how Velo builds the demo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your VeloTwin">
    Choose an AI presenter voice, add context instructions, and set a target video length. Then click Generate Script.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the script">
    Review the AI-generated scene-by-scene script. Edit the narration for each scene, then click Go, Make my Velo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the agent record">
    Velo's AI agent takes over — it walks through your files, following the script, and records the demo automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and share">
    Fine-tune your demo in the editor, add a VeloTwin face, then publish and share.
  </Step>
</Steps>
