In Velo, your script doesn\'t just provide the text, it drives the
entire performance. Anytime you refine your narration or adjust the
pacing, you'll need to regenerate the script to ensure your **Digital
Twin** and the browser agent are in perfect sync.

Here is how to edit your project script.

### **Editing Your Video Narration**

Velo is designed for a \"one-shot\" perfect take. When you modify your
text, the system must re-map those words to your voice clone and the
browser\'s actions.

- **Scene-Specific Updates**

> If you have only adjusted the wording in one small section, you can
> update just that specific Scene Card. This is a fast way to fix a
> single sentence without re-processing the rest of the project

- **Edit limits**

> You get an option to add only 20% more new words at max. We have this
> limit as the actions and length changes which does not allow for the
> most perfect Velo. If you want to add more words, it's best you redo
> the assisted recording.
