Once you have completed Assisted Recording, a Script is generated and
you will be taken to the Script editor. Velo uses mouse movements,
microphone input and additional prompts to draft a script.

With the script editor, you can:

1.  Review the Scene Cards:

> Your walkthrough is broken down into small, manageable scenes. Each
> card shows the scene number, the exact timestamp (for example,
> 00:00--00:15), the visual action, and the script.

![Description](/images/script-generation/script-generation-steps/1.png)

2.  Direct Ending:

> You can start typing right in the narration box whenever you want to
> make a change. There's no need to hunt for an \"Edit\" button, the
> boxes are already open and ready for your updates. If you don\'t like
> your changes, just hit the Reset button to go back to the original
> AI-generated script instantly.

![Description](/images/script-generation/script-generation-steps/2.png)
