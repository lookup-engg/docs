After your recording is saved, Velo takes you to the **Select Voice** page. This is where you preview your recording and pick the AI presenter - called a **VeloTwin** - whose voice will narrate your Velo.

{/* Screenshot: Select Voice page */}

## Previewing your recording

At the top of the page, you'll see a video player with your raw screen recording. Play it back to confirm everything captured correctly before you commit to generating.

Below the player, the **usage bar** shows:
- How many Velo Minutes this recording will consume
- How many Velo Minutes you have remaining on your plan

## Selecting a VeloTwin

The **Select Your VeloTwin** section shows your available AI presenters. Each card displays a thumbnail and the presenter's name with their accent (for example, "Drew - American" or "Viraj - Indian").

Click the play icon on any card to hear a voice preview before selecting. Choose the one that best fits your brand and audience.

<Warning>
  Your VeloTwin **cannot be changed after Velo generation**. Choose carefully - this selection is locked once you click "Go, Make my Velo."
</Warning>

### Public VeloTwins vs. My VeloTwins

The **Public VeloTwins** tab shows Velo's built-in library of AI presenters. The **My VeloTwins** tab shows any custom VeloTwins you've created using your own voice and likeness. Learn how to create one in the [Creating VeloTwin](/creating-velotwin/overview) section.

<Note>
  Selecting a VeloTwin here sets the **narration voice** only. If you want to add the presenter's face as a camera overlay in your video, you can do that in the editor after generation. See [Add VeloTwin Face](/capture-screen/add-velotwin-face).
</Note>

## Generating your Velo

Once you've selected a VeloTwin, click **Go, Make my Velo →**.

Velo will process your recording through several stages - you'll see status messages like **Preparing your file…** and **Detecting zoom regions…** with a progress percentage. This typically takes under a minute for short recordings.

When processing is complete, you'll land on the preview page where you can watch your generated Velo and head into the editor.
