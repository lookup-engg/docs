Capture Screen Recording is Velo's recommended creation method. You record your product live — navigating through it naturally — and Velo transforms that recording into a polished, AI-narrated demo with synchronized zooms, a VeloTwin voice, and a professional background.

This method works for any software you can show on screen: web apps, desktop tools, dashboards, internal tools, and more.

## When to use it

Capture Screen Recording is the best choice when:

- You want to demo a **live, interactive product** with real navigation
- Your product requires **authentication** or can't be accessed via a public URL
- You want **precise control** over what gets recorded — exactly what you show is what ends up in the demo
- You're recording a **desktop application** or anything outside a browser

## What you'll need

- A **Velo account**
- The **Velo Chrome Extension** installed ([see setup guide](/chrome-extension/installation))
- The product or app you want to demo, ready to go

## The flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up and name your recording">
    Open the Capture Screen Recording modal from the dashboard, name your project, and click Continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what to share">
    Chrome will ask you to select a tab, window, or your entire screen to capture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record your walkthrough">
    Navigate through your product naturally. Velo captures everything in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your VeloTwin voice">
    After stopping the recording, choose the AI presenter voice that will narrate your demo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and generate">
    Review the auto-generated script in the editor, make edits, then generate your Velo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and share">
    Fine-tune your demo in the editor — adjust zooms, add a face, tweak audio — then publish and share.
  </Step>
</Steps>
