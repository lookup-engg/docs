## What is Velo?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Velo and what does it do?">
    Velo is an AI-powered video messaging tool. You record a guided narration
    of your product, workflow, or idea once, and Velo transforms it into a
    polished, share-ready video message featuring your AI avatar - without you
    needing to turn on a camera or nail a perfect take.

    If you've got something to show, just Velo it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Velo different from a screen recorder like Loom?">
    Traditional screen recorders require you to appear on camera and deliver a
    flawless take every time. Velo is different - you narrate a quick
    walkthrough, and Velo's browser agent re-records it cleanly using your AI
    avatar and voice clone, producing an awesome video every time with no
    camera anxiety or retakes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What kinds of content can I create with Velo?">
    Velo works for anyone who needs to communicate visually. Common use cases
    include:

    - **Sales** - polished video pitches to prospects
    - **Product** - feature walkthroughs and team updates
    - **Support & CS** - onboarding guides and training videos
    - **Marketing** - launch videos and product content
    - **Founders** - investor updates and product demos
    - **Educators** - courses, lectures, and walkthroughs
    - **Remote teams** - async work updates that replace follow-up meetings
    - **Creators** - tutorials, reviews, and content for YouTube and social media

    You can build videos from a live website, an existing screen recording, or
    static files like PDFs and slide decks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create a video without turning on my camera?">
    Yes. Velo builds a hyper-realistic AI avatar from a single photo and a
    short voice recording. Once your VeloTwin is set up, you never need to
    appear on camera again for any future video.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it transparent to use an AI avatar in videos?">
    Velo generates realistic AI avatars, so it is generally not obvious to
    viewers that an AI was used. That said, you are always free to disclose
    this to your audience as you see fit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo work on Mac and Windows?">
    Yes. Velo runs in the browser and works on any operating system. You just
    need Google Chrome and the Velo Chrome extension installed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo support languages other than English?">
    Not yet. Velo currently supports English only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do I need to get started?">
    You need:

    - A desktop computer
    - Google Chrome
    - The Velo Chrome extension installed
    - A working microphone (for VeloTwin setup and narrating recordings)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo work on mobile?">
    You need a desktop to record and create videos. Once a video is published,
    your Velo link is fully responsive and looks great on any phone or tablet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I install the Velo Chrome extension?">
    Go to the Chrome Web Store, search for **Velo Companion**, and click
    **Add to Chrome**. Pin it to your browser bar for quick access. You only
    need to do this once.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What permissions does the Chrome extension need?">
    The Velo extension requires microphone and screen recording permissions. If
    it is not working, go to `chrome://extensions`, find Velo Companion, and
    make sure the correct permissions are enabled. These are required before
    you can begin recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a VeloTwin?">
    A VeloTwin is your personal AI avatar - it looks like you and sounds like
    you. It is built from a single photo and a short voice recording. Once
    created, your VeloTwin is saved to your account and can present any future
    Velo video instantly without you ever re-recording yourself.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create my VeloTwin?">
    Creating a VeloTwin is a one-time two-step process. First, you take a photo
    using your camera following a 3-second countdown. Then you read a short
    training script naturally so Velo can clone your voice. Once submitted,
    your VeloTwin is ready to use on any project immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is setting up a VeloTwin a one-time process?">
    Yes. You set it up once and it is instantly available for every future Velo
    video you create.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my VeloTwin sound like me, including my accent?">
    Yes. Velo clones your actual voice rather than using a generic synthesised
    voice. It captures your specific tonality, cadence, and accent so your
    VeloTwin sounds like the real you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use a pre-built avatar if I haven't created my own VeloTwin?">
    Yes. Velo provides a library of public avatars you can select immediately -
    while your VeloTwin is being set up, or if you prefer not to create a
    personal one. Access to all public avatars is included on the Pro plan and
    above.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my voice recording quality is poor during VeloTwin setup?">
    After recording, Velo automatically assesses the audio quality. If it is
    not usable, you will be prompted to retake it with specific feedback on how
    to improve. Recording in a quiet space with your best available microphone
    gives the best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recording & Script

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the ways to create a Velo?">
    There are four creation methods:

    - **Capture screen recording** - Record your screen live using the Chrome
      extension as you walk through your product.
    - **Upload a recording** - Upload an existing raw video file (MP4 and
      other formats). Velo writes a new script from scratch and syncs
      everything into a polished video.
    - **Upload PDFs and PNGs** - Import a static slide deck or images as the
      visual base for your video.
    - **Paste a link** - Provide a URL and Velo's browser agent visits the
      live page directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload an existing video file to Velo?">
    Yes. You can upload an existing raw screen recording to Velo. Velo will
    write a new script from scratch, sync your AI avatar to it, and deliver a
    polished, share-ready video - without you needing to re-record anything.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create a Velo from a PDF or slide deck?">
    Yes. Velo supports PDFs and image-based slide decks as the visual
    foundation for your video. This is ideal for quarterly business reviews,
    pitch decks, and internal training guides.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does assisted recording work?">
    You click through your product or file and narrate what is happening in
    your own words, as if explaining it to a colleague. Velo captures your
    voice and mouse movements to understand your product flow, then generates
    a script and a clean re-recorded video using your AI avatar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does the Restart button do during recording?">
    The Restart button clears your current audio and lets you redo the entire
    assisted recording from the beginning. Note that Restart resets the full
    take - it cannot re-record a single section. If you stumble on one phrase,
    use Restart to redo the whole walkthrough cleanly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo save my progress if my internet cuts out?">
    Velo has auto-save features that protect your session during recording.
    However, a significant connection drop at the moment you click Finish
    Session can prevent data from reaching Velo's servers. It is best to
    confirm a stable connection before finalising your walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit the script before the video is generated?">
    Yes. After recording, Velo generates a script broken into Scene Cards. You
    can edit the narration in any Scene Card directly. If you want to discard
    your changes, click Reset to return to the original AI-generated version.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how much I can change the script?">
    You can remove as much text as you like. However, you can only add up to
    20% more words than the original script. Adding significantly more words
    affects the browser agent's timing and video quality. If you need to add
    substantially more content, it is best to redo the assisted recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Scene Card?">
    A Scene Card is one segment of your Velo script. Each card shows the scene
    number, timestamp, the visual action the browser agent will perform, and
    the narration your AI avatar will deliver. Scene Cards are the building
    blocks of every Velo video.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my microphone wasn't working during recording?">
    If Velo did not have microphone access, or if your hardware was muted, it
    will not have the audio data needed to generate a script. Always check your
    microphone settings before starting, and ensure the Chrome extension has
    the correct permissions granted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did Velo generate wrong or nonsensical words in my script?">
    This typically happens when technical jargon is mumbled or mispronounced
    during recording, causing the AI to substitute similar-sounding words. Use
    the Restart button to redo the walkthrough, speak clearly at a natural
    pace, and add context in the Additional Instructions field before
    generating.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Editing & Customisation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit my Velo after it has been generated?">
    Yes. Click **Edit Video** to open the Velo editor, where you can customise
    your video extensively. All changes are saved automatically as you work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can I customise in the editor?">
    The editor includes controls for:

    - **Background** - wallpaper, gradient, solid colour, or custom image;
      plus blur, padding, rounded corners, border/inset, and shadow
    - **Avatar** - position, size, shape, and zoom behaviour
    - **Cursor** - style, size, click effects, and sounds
    - **Audio** - background music, narration volume, and master volume
    - **Zoom** - auto zoom (follows cursor) or manual zoom (locked to a region)
    - **Effects** - Callout, Spotlight, and Mask overlays
    - **Cards** - intro card with your name, role, and video description
    - **Logo** - placement, size, and positioning
    - **Scripts** - view and edit your narration transcript
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add my company logo to a Velo?">
    Yes. Toggle the **Logo** switch in the editor to overlay your logo on the
    video. Use the positioning grid to snap it to any corner, and the Logo Size
    slider to scale it up or down.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hide sensitive information in my video?">
    Yes. The **Masks** feature lets you cover specific areas of your screen
    recording - such as passwords, private emails, or confidential data - so
    viewers only see what you intend to share. You can apply multiple masks at
    different points throughout the timeline.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add background music to my Velo?">
    Yes. Enable the **Background Music** toggle in the Audio panel to choose
    from Velo's built-in music library. You can adjust music volume
    independently from your narration. Uploading custom music files is not yet
    supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I zoom into specific parts of the screen recording?">
    Yes. Velo supports **Auto zoom**, where the camera follows your cursor
    automatically, and **Manual zoom**, where you lock the camera onto a
    specific area. You can also enable **Make Smaller during zoom** so your
    avatar automatically shrinks during a zoom and never blocks the highlighted
    content.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sharing & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I share my Velo?">
    Click **Publish** in the editor or on the preview page. Once rendering
    completes, a shareable link is generated. Copy and send it directly to your
    audience - viewers can watch in their browser with no install required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I download my Velo as a video file?">
    Yes. After publishing, a **Download** option is available alongside your
    shareable link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you store my avatar and voice data?">
    Yes. Your VeloTwin and voice clone are stored securely on Velo's servers so
    you can reuse them across all future projects. If you delete your account,
    your VeloTwin and all associated training data are permanently erased.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I delete my account?">
    Your VeloTwin, voice clone, and all training data are permanently deleted
    from Velo's systems when you delete your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Velo cost?">
    Velo has three plans:

    - **Free** - \$0/month. Includes 15 Velo minutes per month, full editor
      access, and 1 video with avatar per month. Downloads are watermarked by
      default (watermark can be removed for \$9.99).
    - **Pro** - $20/month. Includes 60 Velo minutes per month, up to 3 minutes
      of avatar video per month, access to all public avatars, no watermarks on
      downloads, 3 script retries per video, 3 voice regenerations per video,
      and 5 link-to-video conversions per month.
    - **Ultra** - $200/month. Includes 600 Velo minutes per month, up to 30 minutes
      of avatar video per month, your own custom VeloTwins, 10 script retries per video,
      10 voice regenerations per video, and unlimited link-to-video.

    See the [Pricing](/pricing/pricing) page for full details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are Velo Minutes?">
    Velo Minutes are the unit of usage on Velo. Each plan includes a monthly
    allowance. Minutes are consumed based on the length of the recordings and
    videos you generate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes. The Free plan gives you 15 Velo minutes per month, full editor access,
    and 1 video with avatar per month - no credit card required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
