### **1. Install the Extension from the Chrome Web Store**

The Velo Chrome extension (**Velo Companion**) enables screen recording
directly from your browser - it is required for the **Capture Screen
Recording** creation method.

- Open the Chrome Web Store and search for **Velo Companion**, or go
  directly to the extension page.

- Click **Add to Chrome**, then click **Add extension** in the
  confirmation dialog.

- Chrome will install the extension and show a confirmation once it
  is ready.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/installation/1.png)

### **2. Pin the Extension to Your Browser Bar**

Pinning the extension keeps it visible in your toolbar so you can
access it quickly.

- Click the **puzzle piece icon** in the top-right corner of Chrome to
  open the extensions menu.

- Find **Velo Companion** in the list.

- Click the **pin icon** next to it to pin it to your browser bar.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/installation/2.png)

### **3. Sign In to the Extension**

After pinning, click the Velo Companion icon in your toolbar.

- Sign in with your Velo account credentials.

- Once signed in, you are ready to start capturing screen recordings.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/installation/3.png)

<Note>
  The Velo extension only needs to be installed once. After that, you
  can start recordings directly from the
  [make-a-velo](https://app.usevelo.ai/make-a-velo) page - no need to
  open the extension popup each time.
</Note>
