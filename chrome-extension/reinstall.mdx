If you are experiencing issues with the Velo Companion extension —
such as recordings not starting, the modal not appearing, or the
extension becoming unresponsive — a full reinstall usually resolves
the problem.

### **1. Remove the Extension**

- Type `chrome://extensions` in your browser's address bar and press
  Enter.

- Find **Velo Companion** in the list.

- Click **Remove** and confirm the removal in the dialog.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/reinstall/1.png)

### **2. Fully Quit and Restart Chrome**

After removing the extension, close Chrome completely — do not just
close the tab or window.

- **Mac** — Press `Cmd + Q` to quit Chrome, then reopen it.

- **Windows** — Right-click the Chrome icon in the taskbar or system
  tray and choose **Exit**, then reopen it.

This ensures any cached extension data is fully cleared before
reinstalling.

### **3. Reinstall Velo Companion**

Once Chrome has restarted:

- Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for **Velo Companion**.

- Click **Add to Chrome**, then click **Add extension**.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/reinstall/2.png)

### **4. Pin the Extension and Sign Back In**

After reinstalling:

- Click the **puzzle piece icon** in your toolbar and pin **Velo
  Companion** for quick access.

- Open the extension and sign back in with your Velo account.

You should now be able to start recordings normally from the
[make-a-velo](https://app.usevelo.ai/make-a-velo) page.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/reinstall/3.png)
