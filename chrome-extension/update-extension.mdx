Manually update your Velo chrome extension to the latest version

Keeping your Velo Chrome extension up to date ensures you always have
the latest features, improvements, and bug fixes. Updates are usually
automatic, but if you want to make sure you're on the latest version (or
force an update right away), you can follow these steps:

1.  **Open the extensions page :-** In your browser, type
    chrome://extensions in the address bar and hit Enter.

2.  **Enable Developer Mode :-** On the top-right corner of the page,
    turn on Developer Mode.

3.  **Update your extensions :-** Click on the Update button. Chrome
    will refresh all your extensions, including Velo, to their latest
    versions.
