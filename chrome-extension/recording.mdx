### **1. Starting a Screen Recording**

Make sure the Velo Companion extension is installed before recording.
Then:

- Go to [app.usevelo.ai/make-a-velo](https://app.usevelo.ai/make-a-velo).

- Click the **Capture screen recording** card (marked as
  **Recommended**).

- A recording modal will appear with an auto-generated project name
  and a **REC** indicator showing **Recording in progress**.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/recording/1.png)

### **2. Naming Your Project**

Before or during the recording, you can rename the project:

- Click the **Name the Project** field in the recording modal.

- Type a descriptive name for your demo (e.g., "Onboarding
  Walkthrough" or "Q3 Feature Demo").

- The name will be used as the title for your Velo once it is
  generated.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/recording/2.png)

### **3. Recording Your Demo**

Once the recording is live, walk through your product naturally:

- Navigate to the screens and features you want to show.

- Speak clearly into your microphone as you walk through the product
  — your narration will be used to generate the AI script.

- There is no time limit, but keep your walkthrough focused and
  concise for the best results.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/recording/3.png)

### **4. Stopping the Recording**

When you have covered everything you want to show:

- Click **Stop Recording** in the modal.

- Velo will process your recording and take you to the **VeloTwin
  Setup** page.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/recording/4.png)

### **5. After Stopping — VeloTwin Setup**

After the recording stops, you land on the VeloTwin Setup page:

- The recording duration and its cost in **Velo Minutes** are shown
  at the top.

- Select a **VeloTwin** to present your demo.

- Click **Go, Make my Velo** to begin generation.

From here, Velo handles everything — AI script writing, agent
recording, and video rendering — automatically.

![Description](/images/chrome-extension/recording/5.png)
