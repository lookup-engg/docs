description: ""
---

You can share your Velo with anyone via link.

Once your Velo is generated, click on **Share** button on top right
corner.

![Description](/images/sharing-video/share-via-link/1.png)

In Viewer settings, you can select if you want to get View Counts and
Show Publishing Date. Select a thumbnail for your video and you are good
to go.

![Description](/images/sharing-video/share-via-link/2.png)

From here you can copy the link and share it manually or just select the
means through which you want to share your Velo.

![Description](/images/sharing-video/share-via-link/3.png)
