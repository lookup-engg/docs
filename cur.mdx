### **How to hide cursor in Velo**

- Toggle the **Hide cursor** switch at the top of the menu to completely remove the mouse from your video.

### **How to change the cursor in Velo**

- In the **Cursor Style** section, click on any of the provided icons to change your pointer to a classic arrow, a hand, or a high-contrast style.

### **How to change cursor size in Velo**

- Adjust the **Cursor Size** slider to make your pointer larger or smaller.
- The current percentage (e.g., **50%**) is displayed directly below the slider for precision.

### **How to hide static cursor in my Velo**

- Enable the **Hide cursor if not moving** toggle to ensure the mouse disappears when it is idle.
- You can also adjust the **Hide not moving cursor after** slider to control exactly how many seconds of inactivity should pass before the cursor fades away.

### **How to add rotate effects in my Velo**

- Expand the **Rotation** menu and move the **Rotate Cursor** slider to set a fixed angle.
- Enable **Smart Rotate** to make the cursor slightly tilt automatically as it moves, giving it a more natural "chasing" look.

### **How to add click effect and sound in my Velo**

- **Click Effects:** Open the **Click Effect** dropdown and select **Circle** or **Ripple** to visually highlight every time a click occurs.
- **Click Sounds:** Open the **Click Sound** dropdown to choose an audio cue like **Magic Mouse™** or **MX Master™**.
- **Volume:** Use the **Click Sound Volume** slider to adjust how loud these clicks are in your final video
