### **1. When the Download Option Appears**

Velo offers a download option in two situations:

- **After a successful publish** — Once your Velo is rendered and
  published, you can download the video file in addition to sharing
  it via link.

- **When publishing fails** — If the upload step fails (for example,
  due to a Mux upload error), Velo falls back to a
  **download-only** state. In this case, no shareable link is
  generated, but you can still download the rendered video file.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/download/1.png)

### **2. Downloading Your Velo**

To download your video:

- Click the **Download** button in the share panel.

- The rendered video file will be saved to your computer.

- You can then upload or share the file through any channel you prefer,
  such as email, Slack, Google Drive, or a video hosting platform.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/download/2.png)

### **3. What to Do If Publishing Keeps Failing**

If the upload consistently fails and you are only able to download:

- Check your internet connection and try publishing again.

- If the issue persists, download the file and share it manually
  while you reach out to support.

<Note>
  Downloaded files are fully rendered and contain all layers of your
  Velo — background, face overlay, effects, and audio — just like the
  hosted version.
</Note>
