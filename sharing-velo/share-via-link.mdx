### **1. Where to Find the Publish Button**

The **Publish** button is accessible from two places:

- The **editor page** - in the top-right area of the editor while you
  are still making changes.

- The **preview page** - after you have finished editing and are
  reviewing the final result.

Click **Publish** from either location to begin generating your
shareable Velo.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/share-via-link/1.png)

### **2. What Happens When You Click Publish**

After clicking **Publish**, Velo begins rendering your video. This
includes compositing all layers - your screen recording, VeloTwin face,
background, effects, and audio - into a single output file.

- Rendering takes a moment depending on the length of your video.

- Do not close the tab while rendering is in progress.

- A progress indicator will show you the current status.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/share-via-link/2.png)

### **3. Getting Your Shareable Link**

Once rendering completes successfully, your Velo is published and a
shareable link becomes available.

- Copy the link and send it directly to your audience.

- Recipients can watch the Velo in their browser without needing to
  install anything.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/share-via-link/3.png)

### **4. Link Settings**

After publishing, you will see link settings alongside your shareable
URL. These let you control how your Velo is accessed and presented to
viewers.

- Adjust settings such as access controls, visibility, and display
  preferences directly from the share panel.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/share-via-link/4.png)

### **5. What If Publishing Fails?**

Occasionally the video upload step may fail (for example, due to a
network issue). If this happens:

- Velo will show a **download-only** state instead of a shareable
  link.

- You can still download the rendered video file to your computer and
  share it manually.

- See the [Download](/sharing-velo/download) page for instructions.

![Description](/images/sharing-velo/share-via-link/5.png)
