### **1. Viewing Your Narration Script**

Open the **Scripts** panel from the right sidebar to see the full
narration script that was generated for your Velo. The script is
displayed as a scrollable transcript, organized to reflect the flow of
your Velo.

- Each line of narration corresponds to a segment of the video.

- Use this panel to review exactly what was said at each point in
  your recording.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/scripts/1.png)

### **2. Editing the Script**

You can make changes to your narration script directly in the Scripts
panel.

- Click any line of text to place your cursor and begin editing.

- If you already locked the script, then you won't be able to edit it.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/scripts/2.png)

### **3. How Script Changes Affect Your Video**

Editing the script text updates the narration content, but does not
automatically re-generate the AI voice. Use script editing to keep your
documentation accurate and in sync with what viewers hear.

- If you need the voice to reflect your changes, you will need to
  click on Generate Voice button.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/scripts/3.png)
