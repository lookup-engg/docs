### **1. What Is the Difference Between Cut and Crop?**

Velo provides two distinct tools for trimming your recording:

- **Cut (✂)** — Splits the video at the exact position of the playhead,
  dividing it into two separate segments. Use this to remove a middle
  section of your recording.

- **Crop (⊡)** — Opens a modal where you can trim the start and end of
  your entire recording by dragging handles inward. Use this to remove
  unwanted content from the beginning or end.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cut-and-crop/1.png)

### **2. Using Cut to Split Your Video**

The Cut tool splits the video at the current playhead position.

- Move the playhead to the exact moment where you want to make the
  cut.

- Click the **✂ Cut** button in the toolbar.

- The video is divided into two segments at that point.

- Select either segment to move, delete, or trim it independently.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cut-and-crop/2.png)

### **3. Removing a Section Using Cut**

To remove a middle portion of your video:

1. Move the playhead to the start of the unwanted section and click
   **Cut**.

2. Move the playhead to the end of the unwanted section and click
   **Cut** again.

3. Select the segment between the two cuts and delete it.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cut-and-crop/3.png)

### **4. Using Crop to Trim the Start or End**

The Crop tool lets you trim from either end of the full recording.

- Click the **⊡ Crop** button in the toolbar to open the crop modal.

- Drag the left handle inward to trim the beginning of the video.

- Drag the right handle inward to trim the end.

- The preview updates in real time so you can see exactly where the
  video will start and end.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cut-and-crop/4.png)

### **5. Setting a Custom Aspect Ratio in the Crop Modal**

Inside the crop modal, an **Aspect Ratio** dropdown is available.

- The default is **Any**, which lets you crop freely without
  constraints.

- Select a specific ratio to lock the crop region to a fixed proportion
  as you adjust the handles.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cut-and-crop/5.png)
