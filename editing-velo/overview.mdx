### **1. What Is the Velo Editor?**

The Velo editor is where you finalize your Velo before sharing it. After
your Velo is generated, you are taken directly into the editor to make
adjustments.

- The **right sidebar** contains all the settings panels for
  customizing your video's appearance, content, and behavior.

- The **bottom timeline** lets you view and interact with the different
  tracks that make up your video.

- The **toolbar** at the top of the timeline gives you quick access to
  playback, cutting, cropping, and zoom controls.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/overview/1.png)

### **2. Right Sidebar Panels**

The right sidebar is organized into dedicated panels. Click any icon to
open its settings:

- **Screen** - Change the background style, output format, blur, and
  padding around your recording.

- **Scripts** - View and edit the narration script that was used to
  generate your Velo.

- **Insert** - Add text boxes, images, shapes, arrows, callouts,
  spotlights, masks, and zoom blocks to your video.

- **Cards** - Enable and customize an intro card that plays at the
  beginning of your video.

- **Face** - Toggle your VeloTwin face overlay on or off, reposition
  it, and adjust its size and shape.

- **Audio** - Control background music, master volume, and narration
  volume.

- **Settings** - Update the video title, description, and logo.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/overview/2.png)

### **3. The Timeline**

The timeline at the bottom of the editor shows all the tracks in your
project. Each track controls a different layer of your video:

- **Camera** - Your VeloTwin face overlay segments.

- **Video** - The screen recording waveform and playback bar.

- **Zoom** - Zoom blocks that control when and how the camera zooms in.

- **Masking** - Mask segments that hide sensitive areas of the screen.

- **Spotlight** - Spotlight blocks that dim the surrounding area to
  focus attention on a region.

- **Callout** - Callout blocks that draw a highlighted box around a
  specific area on screen.

Drag the playhead to scrub through your video. Click any block in the
timeline to select it and open its settings in the right sidebar.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/overview/3.png)

### **4. The Toolbar**

The toolbar sits above the timeline and contains the following controls:

- **Undo / Redo** - Step backwards or forwards through your edit
  history.

- **Playback controls** - Play, pause, and skip through your video.

- **Cut (✂)** - Splits the video at the current playhead position.

- **Crop (⊡)** - Opens the crop modal to trim the start and end of your
  recording.

- **Timecode** - Displays the current playhead position and total
  duration.

- **Zoom slider** - Adjusts the horizontal zoom level of the timeline
  for precision editing.

- **Fullscreen** - Expands the preview to fill the screen.
