### **1. Adjusting the Camera Position on Your Velo**

- Use the **Camera Position** grid to snap your avatar to any of the
  nine standard positions on the screen.

- Simply click a circle on the grid to instantly move your camera to
  that corner, side, or the center of the frame.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/camera/1.png)

### **2. Adjusting Camera Video Resolution**

- Use the **Camera Size** slider to scale your avatar up or down.

- Moving the slider to the right increases your visibility, while
  sliding it to the left makes the camera more discreet.

### **3. Adjusting the Roundness of Your Camera Video**

- Adjust the **Roundness** slider to modify the corner radius of your
  camera frame.

- Sliding this to the right will gradually turn a square frame into a
  rounded one.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/camera/2.png)

### **4. Adjusting the Video Shape of Your Camera Feed**

- In the **Shape** section, you can quickly toggle between three
  presets:

  - **Original**: Your default captured frame.

  - **Square**: A perfectly even four-sided frame.

  - **Round**: A circular \"bubble\" look.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/camera/3.png)

### **5. Zoom Effects on Camera Video: Is It Possible?**

Yes, dynamic zoom effects can be incorporated directly into the output
of your camera video stream.

- Velo includes an intelligent **Camera Size During zoom** feature.

- By enabling the **Make Smaller during zoom** toggle, your camera will
  automatically shrink when the video zooms into your screen recording.
  This ensures your avatar never blocks the content being highlighted.

###   6. **Camera Zoom Effect Customization**

- You can control exactly how much your camera shrinks during a zoom
  event.

- Use the **Size during Zoom** slider to set the specific scale for your
  avatar when a zoom is active.

- Click **Reset** at any time to return to the default zoom behavior.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/camera/4.png)
