### **1. What Is the Callout Effect?**

The Callout effect draws a highlighted box around a specific area of
your screen, making it easy for viewers to see exactly where to look.
Unlike Spotlight, Callout adds a visible bordered region rather than
dimming the surroundings.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/1.png)

### **2. Adding a Callout to Your Velo**

To add a Callout:

- Open the **Insert** panel from the right sidebar.

- Under the **Effects** section, click **Callout**.

- A draggable callout region will appear on the canvas and a
  corresponding block will be added to the **Callout** track in the
  timeline.

- Drag and resize the region on the canvas to frame the area you want
  to highlight.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/2.png)

### **3. Adjusting the Callout Scale**

The **Scale** slider controls the overall size of the callout region
relative to the canvas.

- Slide right to increase the scale of the callout.

- Slide left to shrink it.

This is distinct from dragging the region on the canvas — Scale adjusts
the proportional size of the callout box itself.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/3.png)

### **4. Adjusting Background Opacity**

The **Background Opacity** slider controls how visible the fill color
inside the callout region appears.

- A higher value creates a more solid filled box.

- A lower value makes the fill transparent, showing only the border.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/4.png)

### **5. Changing the Callout Background Color**

The **Background Color** picker lets you choose the fill color of the
callout box.

- Click the color swatch to open the picker and select your preferred
  highlight color.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/5.png)

### **6. Rounding the Callout Corners**

Use the **Border Radius** slider to round the corners of your callout
region.

- Slide right for a softer, rounded look.

- Slide left to keep sharp rectangular corners.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/6.png)

### **7. Setting the Callout Duration**

The **Duration** field controls how long the callout remains visible in
your video.

- Enter a specific value or drag the edges of the Callout block
  directly in the timeline to adjust its length.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/callout/7.png)
