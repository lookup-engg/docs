### **1. Updating the Title and Description for your Velo**

You can update your project's identity directly in the **Video
Settings**:

- **Title**: Click the text box under **Title** to rename your project
  (e.g., \"Q3_QBR_Report\").

- **Description**: Use the **Description** field to provide a detailed
  overview of the video\'s content. This metadata helps viewers
  understand the context before they hit play.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/video-settings/1.png)

### **2. Incorporating Your Company Logo into a Video**

To add your branding, simply toggle the **Logo** switch to the \"on\"
position. This will overlay your uploaded logo onto the video canvas,
ensuring every shared Velo link carries your brand identity.

### **3. Customizing Logo Placement**

**Yes.** Once the logo is enabled, you will see a positioning grid with
four arrows. Click on any of the arrows to snap your logo to the
top-left, top-right, bottom-left, or bottom-right corner of the video.

### **4. Adjusting Logo Size**

If your logo feels too dominant or too small, use the **Logo Size**
slider.

- Slide to the right to increase visibility or to the left for a more
  subtle watermark effect.

- The current size percentage (e.g., **50%**) is displayed below the
  slider for easy reference.

- Click **Reset** at any time to return to the default logo dimensions.
