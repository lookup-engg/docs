### **1. Changing the Background Style in Velo**

Open the **Screen** panel from the right sidebar to access background
options. There are three style tabs at the top:

- **Wallpaper** - Choose from uploaded images or built-in presets
  organized into categories such as **macOS** and **Miscellaneous**.

- **Gradient** - Pick a preset gradient or define your own by selecting
  two colors using the color pickers.

- **Color** - Apply a flat solid color using the color picker, hex
  input, or one of the preset swatches.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/background/1.png)

### **2. Setting the Output Format**

Below the style tabs, the **Output Format** dropdown controls the
aspect ratio of the final video. The available options are:

- **16:9** - Standard widescreen, suitable for most presentations and
  embeds.

- **9:16** - Vertical format, ideal for mobile or social media.

- **1:1** - Square format for platforms like LinkedIn.

- **4:3** - Classic presentation format.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/background/2.png)

### **3. Blurring the Background**

When a **Wallpaper** background is active, the **Background Blur**
slider becomes available.

- Drag the slider to the right to increase the blur amount.

- Click **Reset** to return to the original unblurred image.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/background/3.png)

### **4. Adjusting Padding Around the Recording**

The **Padding** slider controls how much space appears between the
screen recording and the edges of the background.

- Slide right to add more breathing room around your product recording.

- Slide left to bring the recording closer to the edges.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/background/4.png)
