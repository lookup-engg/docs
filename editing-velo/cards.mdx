### **1. What Is the Intro Card?**

The Intro Card is an optional title screen that plays at the very
beginning of your Velo before it starts. It displays your name,
role, and a brief description, giving viewers context about what they
are about to watch.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cards/1.png)

### **2. Enabling the Intro Card**

Open the **Cards** panel from the right sidebar. Toggle the **Enable**
switch to turn the intro card on.

- When enabled, a preview of the card appears in the panel so you can
  see how it will look.

- Toggle it off to remove the intro card without deleting your
  settings.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cards/2.png)

### **3. Switching Between Light and Dark Mode**

Use the **Light / Dark** toggle to choose the visual style of the intro
card.

- **Light** - A bright white card with dark text, suitable for
  professional or minimal presentations.

- **Dark** - A dark-background card with light text, suitable for
  modern or technical Velos.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cards/3.png)

### **4. Setting Your Name and Role**

Fill in the **Name** and **Role** fields to display your identity on the
intro card.

- **Name** - Your full name as you want it to appear to viewers.

- **Role** - Your job title or team, shown beneath your name.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cards/4.png)

### **5. Adding a Title and Description**

The intro card also supports a title and a short description:

- **Title of video** - A headline for your Velo, up to **30
  characters**.

- **Description** - A short summary of what the video covers, up to
  **200 characters**.

Keep both concise - they are displayed in a small area and should be
readable at a glance.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cards/5.png)
