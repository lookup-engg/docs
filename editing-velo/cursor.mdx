### **1. Hiding the cursor in Velo**

- Toggle the **Hide cursor** switch at the top of the menu to completely
  remove the mouse from your video.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cursor/1.png)

### **2. Changing the cursor in Velo**

- In the **Cursor Style** section, click on any of the provided icons to
  change your pointer to a classic arrow, a hand, or a high-contrast
  style.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cursor/2.png)

### **3. Changing cursor size in Velo**

- Adjust the **Cursor Size** slider to make your pointer larger or
  smaller.

- The current percentage (e.g., **50%**) is displayed directly below the
  slider for precision.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cursor/3.png)

### **4. Hiding static cursor in my Velo**

- Enable the **Hide cursor if not moving** toggle to ensure the mouse
  disappears when it is idle.

- You can also adjust the **Hide not moving cursor after** slider to
  control exactly how many seconds of inactivity should pass before the
  cursor fades away.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cursor/4.png)

### **5. Implementing Rotation Effects within Velo**

- Expand the **Rotation** menu and move the **Rotate Cursor** slider to
  set a fixed angle.

- Enable **Smart Rotate** to make the cursor slightly tilt automatically
  as it moves, giving it a more natural \"chasing\" look.

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cursor/5.png)

### **6. Integrating Click Effects and Sound in Velo**

- **Click Effects:** Open the **Click Effect** dropdown and select
  **Circle** or **Ripple** to visually highlight every time a click
  occurs.

- **Click Sounds:** Open the **Click Sound** dropdown to choose an audio
  cue like **Magic Mouse™** or **MX Master™**.

- **Volume:** Use the **Click Sound Volume** slider to adjust how loud
  these clicks are in your final video

![Description](/images/editing-velo/cursor/6.png)
