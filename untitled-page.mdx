---
title: "Frequently Asked Questions:"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if my website is slow? Will the AI keep talking while the page loads?">
    - No, Velo is "browser-aware".
    - This means the agent actually watches the page and waits for it to finish loading before it continues speaking or moving to the next step.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I have a specific accent. Will my Voice Clone sound like me?">
    - Yes! Velo clones your actual audio rather than using a generic robot voice.
    - It captures your specific tonality, cadence, and accent so your Digital Twin sounds like the real you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my outfit or background without a new photo?">
    - You can swap your background (wallpapers, colors, or gradients) instantly in the editor.
    - change your outfit feature will be launched soon.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you store my Avatar or Voice? What if I delete my account?">
    - We keep your data safe so you can keep making videos.
    - However, your data belongs to you; if you delete your account, your Digital Twin and all its training data are permanently erased.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use this on my phone?">
    - You’ll need a desktop to record and edit your videos.
    - Once the video is made, your Velo links are fully responsive and look great on any phone or tablet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my internet cuts out while I’m recording?">
    - Velo has Auto-Save, so you can usually pick up right where you left off.
    - If one part of the recording goes wrong, you can just use the Restart button to try that specific section again without losing the whole project.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much does each video cost?">
    - Velo uses a simple credit system based on how long your video is.
    - You can always see exactly how many credits you have left right in your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit the video after it’s made?">
    No.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can it translate my English recording into another language?">
    No.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I download the file or just share a link?">
    You get the best of both worlds: share a quick Velo Link or download the final MP4 for your own records.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I upload an existing video and let Velo take over?">
    No.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
