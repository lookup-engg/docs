**Assisted Recording** is the collaborative part of the recording a Velo process. Instead of following a strict script, you simply explain your product flow as you click through it, and the tool captures your intent to create a clean video later.

Here is how to use **Assisted Recording** and the best practices for a perfect result.

### **1. How to Record Your Session**

1. **Start the Session:** Once you have your URL or file open, click the **Start Session** button at the bottom of your screen to begin.
2. **Guide the Tool:** Explain what is happening on screen in your own words as you move through the product.
3. **Fix Mistakes on the Fly:** If you stumble or want to re-explain a step, click **Restart** to clear your current audio and try the **complete** assisted recording again. _(Note: Using_ **_Restart_**\_ will not allow you to re-record just one section.)\_
4. **Finish and Process:** Click **Finish Session** when you’re done with assisted recording to let the tool generate your draft script.
