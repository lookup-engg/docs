---
title: "Upload PDFs & PNGs"
description: "Turn your slides, decks, or screenshots into a narrated product walkthrough."
---

Use this method when your content lives in static files — PDFs, slide decks, or images. Upload your files, narrate over them, and Velo's agent turns them into a polished walkthrough video.

**Best for:** Presentations, slide-based demos, feature overviews, or any content that exists as documents or screenshots.

**Requires:** Chrome Extension installed. Supported formats: PDF, PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP (max 100 MB per file, up to 10 files).

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your files">
    On the dashboard click **Generate a Velo**, then select **Upload PDFs & PNGs**. Upload your files and arrange them in the order you want.

    ![Upload files](/images/getting-started/upload-pdfs-pngs/1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Narrate over your slides">
    Velo opens a canvas with your files. Walk through each slide and narrate out loud — just as you would in a live presentation.

    ![Narrate over canvas](/images/getting-started/upload-pdfs-pngs/2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your voice">
    Choose a VeloTwin to narrate the final demo and set any additional instructions for how you want it to sound.

    ![Select voice](/images/getting-started/upload-pdfs-pngs/3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your script">
    Velo generates a script from your narration. Review each scene, make edits, and confirm when ready.

    ![Script review](/images/getting-started/upload-pdfs-pngs/4.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent records the final demo">
    Velo's browser agent navigates your canvas and records the final, polished version of your walkthrough.

    ![Agent recording](/images/getting-started/upload-pdfs-pngs/5.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
