---
title: "Upload a Recording"
description: "Already have a screen recording? Upload it and let Velo turn it into a polished demo."
---

Use this method when you already have a recorded video of your product. Upload it directly — no Chrome Extension needed — and Velo will generate a narrated, studio-quality demo from it.

**Best for:** Teams who already record their screen with other tools, or have existing demo footage they want to repurpose.

**Requires:** A video file in MP4 or MOV format (max 1 GB).

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your recording">
    On the dashboard click **Generate a Velo**, then select **Upload a Recording**. Drag and drop your file or browse to select it.

    ![Upload recording](/images/getting-started/upload-recording/1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview your recording">
    Velo processes your file and shows you a preview. If no voice is detected in the video, you'll see a warning at this step.

    ![Preview recording](/images/getting-started/upload-recording/2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your voice">
    Choose a VeloTwin to narrate the final demo.

    ![Select voice](/images/getting-started/upload-recording/3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit your script">
    The editor opens automatically. Open the **Scripts** panel on the right sidebar to review the generated narration. Edit any line manually or use AI Rewrite to refine it.

    ![Script review in editor Scripts panel](/images/getting-started/upload-recording/4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
