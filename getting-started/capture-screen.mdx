title: "Capture Screen Recording"
description: "Record your screen live while walking through your product."
---

Use this method when you want to record your product in real time. You walk through it, Velo captures everything — screen, cursor, and audio — and turns it into a polished demo.

**Best for:** Web apps, desktop tools, or any live product you can navigate in a browser.

**Requires:** Chrome Extension installed.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a recording">
    On the dashboard click **Generate a Velo**, then select **Capture Screen Recording**. Velo will launch your screen recording via the Chrome Extension.

    ![Start a recording](/images/getting-started/capture-screen/1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record your screen">
    Walk through your product naturally. Speak out loud as you go — Velo captures your audio and on-screen actions together.

    ![Recording in progress](/images/getting-started/capture-screen/2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your voice">
    Once recording stops, preview your clip and choose a VeloTwin to narrate the final demo.

    ![Select voice](/images/getting-started/capture-screen/3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit your script">
    The editor opens automatically. Open the **Scripts** panel on the right sidebar to review the generated narration. Edit any line manually or use AI Rewrite to refine it.

    ![Script review in editor Scripts panel](/images/getting-started/capture-screen/4.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
