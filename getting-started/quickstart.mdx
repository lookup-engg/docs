Create your first Velo (video) in just minutes.

This quickstart guide shows you how to create your first velo in
minutes. By the end, you'll have a polished video ready to share with
your team or customers.

Prerequisite: Before you begin, [create an account](https://www.usevelo.ai/login) and complete
onboarding.

### **1.** **Getting started**

Once you complete the onboarding process, you'll find yourself on the
[Velo Dashboard](https://app.usevelo.ai/).

If you haven\'t installed the Chrome Extension yet, you can find the
installation guide [here](/chrome-extension/installation).

At the center of Home Dashboard, you'll see the option to **Generate a
Velo**, click on it and start creating your first Velo.

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/1.png)

You will see two clear starting points:

- Paste a Link: You can paste your product link to generate the Velo.
  This works best for live websites or web apps where you want the
  browser to move for you.

- Import File:You can use your files to generate a Velo. This is ideal
  for static content like PDFs, slides, or documents.

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/2.png)

### **2. Guide your Velo (Assisted Recording)**

Once you have pasted a link or imported the file, let's understand what
you want to show in the demo.

Walk us through your product, click through your product flow and
explain what is happening as if talking to a colleague.

It would be best if you speak naturally, as Velo captures your voice to
understand the context.

If you stumble anywhere, just hit Restart to start over again and
correct your mistakes in this take.

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/3.png)

### **3. Choose your VeloTwin**

Now let's decide who will be the one representing you on camera.

You can select your VeloTwin. If you have not yet created a VeloTwin,
check out [this guide](/creating-velotwin/steps-to-create) for in-depth tutorial on how to create your
first VeloTwin

If not VeloTwin, you can choose any twin from our library of Public
Avatars.

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/4.png)

### **4. Review the Script and Generate Velo**

Velo uses mouse movements, microphone input and additional prompts to
draft a script.

You will be able to see the script generated for your Velo. Your
walkthrough is broken down into small, manageable scenes. Each card
shows the scene number, the exact timestamp (for example, 00:00--00:15),
the visual action, and the script.

If you want to edit anything, just start typing right in the narration
box wherever you want to make a change. There's no need to hunt for an
"Edit" button, the boxes are already open and ready for your updates. If
you don't like your changes, just hit the Reset button to go back to the
original AI-generated script instantly.

Once the script is finalized, hit **Go, Make my Velo**.

Our browser agent visits your target link and performs every action
exactly as you've laid it out in your walkthrough. It captures a
recording of your product alongside your VeloTwin for a perfect,
studio-quality finish along with syncing your audio.

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/5.png)

### **5. Help browser agent to record**

Once you click on **Go, Make my Velo**, a new tab will open
automatically and it will navigate you to the link or file you uploaded.

Our agent will start recording your screen. You will be able to see a
banner on the bottom of the screen which has an option to Stop
Recording.

If you click on Stop Recording, the tab will get close and you will be
brought back to the previous page.

Once the recording finishes, you\'re all set, your first Velo will be
ready!

### **6. Edit your Velo**

If you want to make any changes click on **Edit Video**, you will be
taken to the editor. Here, you can edit your Velo to make it more
personalized. It gives you many options like adding your logo, adjusting
its position and size, aligning Velo to your brand colours, etc.

All the changes are saved on the go, if you want to revert them just
reset the feature you changed.

Once you are done, hit **Render**. That's it

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/6.png)

### **7. Share with team or customers**

Now your Velo is ready to be shared with your team members or customers.

From top-right click on Share and create a public link. Share this
public link with everyone.

![Description](/images/getting-started/quickstart/7.png)
