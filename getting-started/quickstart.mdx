## Prerequisites

Before you begin:

1. [Create a Velo account](https://app.usevelo.ai/auth/sign-up) and complete onboarding.
2. [Install the Chrome Extension](/chrome-extension/installation) — required for most methods.

## Getting started

Once you complete onboarding, you'll land on the **Velo Dashboard**.

Click **Generate a Velo** at the center of the dashboard to begin.

![Velo Dashboard](/images/getting-started/quickstart/1.png)

You'll see four ways to create your Velo. Pick the one that best fits your content:

![Four methods to create a Velo](/images/getting-started/quickstart/2.png)
