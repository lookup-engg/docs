Welcome to Velo Docs. Velo is an AI-native product demo and walkthrough generator that lets you create studio-quality product demos — without turning on your camera or manually editing a video.

## Choose how to create your Velo

There are four ways to get started. Pick the one that fits your content:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card icon="video" title="Capture Screen Recording" href="/getting-started/capture-screen">
    Record your screen live while walking through your product.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="arrow-up-from-bracket" title="Upload a Recording" href="/getting-started/upload-recording">
    Already have a screen recording? Upload it and let Velo do the rest.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="file-pdf" title="Upload PDFs & PNGs" href="/getting-started/upload-pdfs-pngs">
    Turn slides, decks, or screenshots into a narrated walkthrough.
  </Card>
  <Card icon="link" title="Paste a Link" href="/getting-started/paste-a-link">
    Paste a URL, narrate your product, and let Velo's agent record the final demo.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
