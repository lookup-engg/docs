---
title: "Paste a Link"
description: "Paste a URL, narrate your product once, and let Velo's browser agent record the final demo."
---

Use this method when you want to demo a live website or web app. Paste the URL, do a quick narration walkthrough, and Velo's autonomous browser agent will navigate the product and record a clean, studio-quality demo — without you having to record it again.

**Best for:** Live web apps, SaaS products, or any public URL you want to demo.

**Requires:** Chrome Extension installed. The URL must be publicly accessible.

<Note>
  This method is rate-limited based on your plan. Check your plan limits before getting started.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste your link">
    On the dashboard click **Generate a Velo**, then select **Paste a Link**. Enter your project name and the URL you want to demo.

    ![Paste a link](/images/getting-started/paste-a-link/1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Narrate your product">
    Velo opens the URL in a new tab. Walk through your product naturally and narrate what you're showing — just talk as if explaining it to a colleague.

    ![Narrate walkthrough](/images/getting-started/paste-a-link/2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your voice">
    Choose a VeloTwin to narrate the final demo and set any additional instructions for tone or style.

    ![Select voice](/images/getting-started/paste-a-link/3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your script">
    Velo generates a script from your narration. Review each scene, make edits, and confirm when ready.

    ![Script review](/images/getting-started/paste-a-link/4.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent records the final demo">
    Velo's browser agent autonomously navigates your product and records the final, polished demo exactly as scripted.

    ![Agent recording](/images/getting-started/paste-a-link/5.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
