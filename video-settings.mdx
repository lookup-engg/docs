### **1. How do I change the title and description of my Velo?**

You can update your project’s identity directly in the **Video Settings**:

- **Title**: Click the text box under **Title** to rename your project (e.g., "Q3_QBR_Report").
- **Description**: Use the **Description** field to provide a detailed overview of the video's content. This metadata helps viewers understand the context before they hit play.

### **2. How do I add my company logo to the video?**

To add your branding, simply toggle the **Logo** switch to the "on" position. This will overlay your uploaded logo onto the video canvas, ensuring every shared Velo link carries your brand identity.

### **3. Can I change where my logo appears on the screen?**

**Yes.** Once the logo is enabled, you will see a positioning grid with four arrows. Click on any of the arrows to snap your logo to the top-left, top-right, bottom-left, or bottom-right corner of the video.

### **4. How do I adjust the size of my logo?**

If your logo feels too dominant or too small, use the **Logo Size** slider.

- Slide to the right to increase visibility or to the left for a more subtle watermark effect.
- The current size percentage (e.g., **50%**) is displayed below the slider for easy reference.
- Click **Reset** at any time to return to the default logo dimensions.
