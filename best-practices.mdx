### **Best Practices for Your Walkthrough**

To ensure your final video is professional and clear, follow these simple tips:

**For Your Speech:**

- **Speak Naturally:** Talk as if you are explaining the product to a teammate in a live meeting.
- **Be Direct:** Avoid corporate fluff or jargon and focus on practical, simple explanations.

**For Your Screen:**

- **Wait for Page Loads:** Give each page a moment to fully load before you start describing the next action.
- **Center the Action:** Keep your mouse movements steady and centered on the UI elements you are discussing
