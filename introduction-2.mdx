The script editor in Velo allows you to refine your narration and manage your video project with total precision. Since Velo uses your script to drive both the voice and the on-screen movement, editing the text is the fastest way to polish your final video.

Here is how to use the editor to perfect your walkthrough.

### **What you can do with the Script Editor**

- **Review individual Scene Cards**: Every video is broken into scenes that show the visual action and the narration text.
- **Edit narration directly**: Click **Edit Script** to change what your digital twin says without ever having to re-record your voice.
- **Manage visual actions**: View exactly what the browser agent will do, such as clicking a specific button or hovering over a table.
- **Switch your VeloTwin**: Change the digital face and voice representing you for the entire project or specific sections.
