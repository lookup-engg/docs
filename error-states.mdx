---
title: "Error States"
---

Here are the most common failures during assisted recording and how to fix them:

### **1. Audio Quality Issues**

- Background Noise: High levels of ambient noise (like a loud fan or office chatter) can prevent the AI from accurately transcribing your speech into a script.
- Microphone Muting: If Velo didn't have proper microphone access or if your hardware was muted, the tool won't have the audio data needed to understand your product flow.

**Solution:** Always check your Microphone Settings before starting and record in a quiet space.

### **2. Interaction Gaps**

- Moving Too Fast: If you click through a complex flow without speaking or explaining the steps, Velo may struggle to identify the "why" behind your actions, leading to vague Scene Cards.
- Broken Links: If the URL you are recording crashes or fails to load during the session, the browser agent won't be able to map the UI elements correctly.

**Solution**: Speak naturally and give each page a second to load so the agent can "see" the buttons you are clicking.

### **3. Script Generation Errors**

- Unclear Prompts: If the Additional Instructions provided are contradictory or too vague, the AI might generate a script that doesn't match your professional role or audience.
- Complex Jargon: Heavy technical jargon that is mumbled or mispronounced can lead to "hallucinated" words in your draft script.

**Solution:** Use the Restart button if you stumble on a difficult phrase, and provide clear, simple context in the Additional Instructions box.

### **4. Processing Timeouts**

- Internet Interruptions: A significant drop in your internet connection while clicking Finish Session can prevent the recording data from reaching our servers.

**Solution:** Velo has Auto-Save features, but it's best to ensure a stable connection before finalising a walkthrough.
