Invite people to your workspace so they can create and share videos using your team's shared minute pool.

## How to invite members

**Step 1:** Open the workspace dropdown by clicking the workspace name at the top of the sidebar, then select **Invite members**. You can also navigate to **Settings → Members** and click the **Invite** button.

**Step 2:** Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.

**Step 3:** Choose a role — **Admin** or **Member**. You can change roles later.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/workspaces/invite-members-modal.jpg" alt="Invite members modal" />
</Frame>

The invited person will receive an email with a link to join your workspace. Once they accept, they'll appear under the **Members** tab in Settings.

## Managing members

From **Settings → Members**, you can:

- View all current members and their roles
- Switch to the **Invited** tab to see pending invitations
- Search members by name or email
- Change a member's role by clicking the role badge next to their name

## Seat limits

Each plan includes a set number of seats:

| Plan | Seats |
| --- | --- |
| Free | 3 |
| Pro | 5 |
| Ultra | 20 |

The **Members** page shows how many seats are available — for example, "2/3 Seats Available" on the Free plan. If you've reached your seat limit, [upgrade your plan](/settings/plans-billing) to add more.
