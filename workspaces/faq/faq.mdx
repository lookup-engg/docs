## What is Velo?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Velo and what does it do?">
    Velo is an AI-powered video messaging platform for teams. You record a guided narration of your product, workflow, or idea once, and Velo transforms it into a polished, share-ready video message featuring your AI avatar — without you needing to turn on a camera or nail a perfect take.

    If you've got something to show, just Velo it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Velo different from a screen recorder like Loom?">
    Traditional screen recorders require you to appear on camera and deliver a flawless take every time. Velo is different — you narrate a quick walkthrough, and Velo's browser agent re-records it cleanly using your AI avatar and voice clone, producing an awesome video every time with no camera anxiety or retakes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What kinds of content can I create with Velo?">
    Velo works for anyone who needs to communicate visually. Common use cases include:

    - **Sales** - polished video pitches to prospects
    - **Product** - feature walkthroughs and team updates
    - **Support & CS** - onboarding guides and training videos
    - **Marketing** - launch videos and product content
    - **Founders** - investor updates and product demos
    - **Educators** - courses, lectures, and walkthroughs
    - **Remote teams** - async work updates that replace follow-up meetings
    - **Creators** - tutorials, reviews, and content for YouTube and social media

    You can build videos from a live website, an existing screen recording, or static files like PDFs and slide decks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create a video without turning on my camera?">
    Yes. Velo builds a hyper-realistic AI avatar from a single photo and a short voice recording. Once your VeloTwin is set up, you never need to appear on camera again for any future video.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it transparent to use an AI avatar in videos?">
    Velo generates realistic AI avatars, so it is generally not obvious to viewers that an AI was used. That said, you are always free to disclose this to your audience as you see fit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo work on Mac and Windows?">
    Yes. Velo runs in the browser and works on any operating system. You just need Google Chrome and the Velo Chrome extension installed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo support languages other than English?">
    Not yet. Velo currently supports English only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Workspaces & Teams

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a workspace?">
    A workspace is a shared environment where your team creates, manages, and shares videos. Each workspace has its own videos, libraries, brand kit, plan, and shared minute pool. You can think of it as your team's home in Velo.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I belong to multiple workspaces?">
    Yes. You can belong to as many workspaces as you want — for example, one for your company and another for a side project. Each workspace has its own plan and minute pool, and you can switch between them using the workspace dropdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can workspaces be on different plans?">
    Yes. Each workspace has its own plan. You might have one workspace on the Free plan and another on Pro — they are managed independently.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are roles in a workspace?">
    Every workspace member has one of three roles:

    - **Member** — can create videos, publish to libraries, and share externally
    - **Admin** — everything a Member can do, plus invite and manage members, create libraries, and configure the brand kit
    - **Owner** — full control including plans, billing, SSO, and the ability to delete the workspace

    The person who creates the workspace is automatically the Owner.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many people can I add to a workspace?">
    It depends on your plan. Free includes 3 seats, Pro includes 5, and Ultra includes 20. If you need more, upgrade to a higher plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do shared minutes work?">
    All members in a workspace draw from the same pool of Velo minutes. When anyone in the workspace creates a video, it consumes minutes from the shared pool. You can see the remaining balance at the bottom of the sidebar and in **Settings → Usage**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when the workspace runs out of minutes?">
    Video creation is blocked for all workspace members until the cycle resets. On the Free plan, you can also [top up minutes](/settings/plans-billing) to resume immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are libraries?">
    Libraries are collections of published videos that you can organize by topic, team, or audience. They can be public (visible to all workspace members) or private (invite-only). Every workspace starts with a default "General" library.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Brand Kit?">
    The Brand Kit lets you set a default logo, brand color, and CTA button for your workspace. Once configured, your branding is automatically applied to every new video — no manual setup needed per video.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo support SSO / SAML?">
    Yes. SSO and SAML 2.0 are supported via WorkOS on the Ultra plan. The workspace Owner can configure SSO from workspace settings. Common providers like Google, Microsoft, and Okta are supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can teammates see my chat threads?">
    No. Every chat thread is strictly private to the person who created it. No teammate — regardless of role — can view or continue another member's chat. Admins can only see videos that have been explicitly published to a library.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do I need to get started?">
    You need:

    - A desktop computer
    - Google Chrome
    - The Velo Chrome extension installed
    - A working microphone (for VeloTwin setup and narrating recordings)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo work on mobile?">
    You need a desktop to record and create videos. Once a video is published, your Velo link is fully responsive and looks great on any phone or tablet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I install the Velo Chrome extension?">
    Go to the Chrome Web Store, search for **Velo Companion**, and click **Add to Chrome**. Pin it to your browser bar for quick access. You only need to do this once.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What permissions does the Chrome extension need?">
    The Velo extension requires microphone and screen recording permissions. If it is not working, go to `chrome://extensions`, find Velo Companion, and make sure the correct permissions are enabled. These are required before you can begin recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a VeloTwin?">
    A VeloTwin is your personal AI avatar — it looks like you and sounds like you. It is built from a single photo and a short voice recording. Once created, your VeloTwin is saved to your account and can present any future Velo video instantly without you ever re-recording yourself.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create my VeloTwin?">
    Creating a VeloTwin is a one-time two-step process. First, you take a photo using your camera following a 3-second countdown. Then you read a short training script naturally so Velo can clone your voice. Once submitted, your VeloTwin is ready to use on any project immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is setting up a VeloTwin a one-time process?">
    Yes. You set it up once and it is instantly available for every future Velo video you create.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my VeloTwin sound like me, including my accent?">
    Yes. Velo clones your actual voice rather than using a generic synthesised voice. It captures your specific tonality, cadence, and accent so your VeloTwin sounds like the real you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use a pre-built avatar if I haven't created my own VeloTwin?">
    Yes. Velo provides a library of public avatars you can select immediately - while your VeloTwin is being set up, or if you prefer not to create a personal one. Access to all public avatars is included on the Pro plan and above.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my voice recording quality is poor during VeloTwin setup?">
    After recording, Velo automatically assesses the audio quality. If it is not usable, you will be prompted to retake it with specific feedback on how to improve. Recording in a quiet space with your best available microphone gives the best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recording & Script

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the ways to create a Velo?">
    There are four creation methods:

    - **Capture screen recording** - Record your screen live using the Chrome extension as you walk through your product.
    - **Upload a recording** - Upload an existing raw video file (MP4 and other formats). Velo writes a new script from scratch and syncs everything into a polished video.
    - **Upload PDFs and PNGs** - Import a static slide deck or images as the visual base for your video.
    - **Paste a link** - Provide a URL and Velo's browser agent visits the live page directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload an existing video file to Velo?">
    Yes. You can upload an existing raw screen recording to Velo. Velo will write a new script from scratch, sync your AI avatar to it, and deliver a polished, share-ready video - without you needing to re-record anything.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create a Velo from a PDF or slide deck?">
    Yes. Velo supports PDFs and image-based slide decks as the visual foundation for your video. This is ideal for quarterly business reviews, pitch decks, and internal training guides.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does assisted recording work?">
    You click through your product or file and narrate what is happening in your own words, as if explaining it to a colleague. Velo captures your voice and mouse movements to understand your product flow, then generates a script and a clean re-recorded video using your AI avatar.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does the Restart button do during recording?">
    The Restart button clears your current audio and lets you redo the entire assisted recording from the beginning. Note that Restart resets the full take - it cannot re-record a single section. If you stumble on one phrase, use Restart to redo the whole walkthrough cleanly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Velo save my progress if my internet cuts out?">
    Velo has auto-save features that protect your session during recording. However, a significant connection drop at the moment you click Finish Session can prevent data from reaching Velo's servers. It is best to confirm a stable connection before finalising your walkthrough.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit the script before the video is generated?">
    After recording, Velo generates a script. For Paste a Link and Upload a PDF flows, this appears as Scene Cards in the chat before the video is built. For Capture Screen and Upload a Recording flows, you review and edit the script in the editor after the video is generated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how much I can change the script?">
    You can remove as much text as you like. However, you can only add up to 20% more words than the original script. Adding significantly more words affects the browser agent's timing and video quality. If you need to add substantially more content, it is best to redo the assisted recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Scene Card?">
    Scene Cards appear in the Paste a Link and Upload a PDF flows only — not in Capture Screen or Upload a Recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my microphone wasn't working during recording?">
    If Velo did not have microphone access, or if your hardware was muted, it will not have the audio data needed to generate a script. Always check your microphone settings before starting, and ensure the Chrome extension has the correct permissions granted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did Velo generate wrong or nonsensical words in my script?">
    This typically happens when technical jargon is mumbled or mispronounced during recording, causing the AI to substitute similar-sounding words. Use the Restart button to redo the walkthrough, speak clearly at a natural pace, and add context in the Additional Instructions field before generating.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Editing & Customisation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit my Velo after it has been generated?">
    Yes. Click Open full editor in the Quick Actions bar to open the Velo editor, where you can make edits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can I customise in the editor?">
    The editor includes controls for:

    - **Background** - wallpaper, gradient, solid colour, or custom image; plus blur, padding, rounded corners, border/inset, and shadow
    - **Avatar** - position, size, shape, and zoom behaviour
    - **Cursor** - style, size, click effects, and sounds
    - **Audio** - background music, narration volume, and master volume
    - **Zoom** - auto zoom (follows cursor) or manual zoom (locked to a region)
    - **Effects** - Callout, Spotlight, and Mask overlays
    - **Cards** - intro card with your name, role, and video description
    - **Logo** - placement, size, and positioning
    - **Scripts** - view and edit your narration transcript
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add my company logo to a Velo?">
    Yes. Toggle the **Logo** switch in the editor to overlay your logo on the video. Use the positioning grid to snap it to any corner, and the Logo Size slider to scale it up or down. If you've set up a [Brand Kit](/workspaces/brand-kit), your workspace logo is applied automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hide sensitive information in my video?">
    Yes. The **Masks** feature lets you cover specific areas of your screen recording - such as passwords, private emails, or confidential data - so viewers only see what you intend to share. You can apply multiple masks at different points throughout the timeline.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add background music to my Velo?">
    Yes. Enable the **Background Music** toggle in the Audio panel to choose from Velo's built-in music library. You can adjust music volume independently from your narration. Uploading custom music files is not yet supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I zoom into specific parts of the screen recording?">
    Yes. Velo supports **Auto zoom**, where the camera follows your cursor automatically, and **Manual zoom**, where you lock the camera onto a specific area. You can also enable **Make Smaller during zoom** so your avatar automatically shrinks during a zoom and never blocks the highlighted content.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sharing & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I share my Velo?">
    Click Get a sharable link in the chat view, or use the Publish video Quick Action. Once rendered, copy the link and send it to your audience — viewers can watch in their browser with no install required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the difference between Publish and Share?">
    **Publish** adds a video to one or more workspace [libraries](/workspaces/libraries) so your teammates can find it. **Share** generates a public link for anyone — including people outside your workspace. These are independent actions: you can publish without sharing, or share without publishing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I download my Velo as a video file?">
    Yes. After publishing, a **Download** option is available alongside your shareable link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you store my avatar and voice data?">
    Yes. Your VeloTwin and voice clone are stored securely on Velo's servers so you can reuse them across all future projects. If you delete your account, your VeloTwin and all associated training data are permanently erased.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I delete my account?">
    Your VeloTwin, voice clone, and all training data are permanently deleted from Velo's systems when you delete your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Velo cost?">
    Velo has three plans:

    - **Free** - \$0/month. Includes 15 Velo minutes per month, 3 seats, full editor access, and 1 video with avatar per month. Downloads are watermarked by default (watermark can be removed for \$9.99).
    - **Pro** - \$49/month. Includes 60 Velo minutes per month, 5 seats, up to 3 minutes of avatar video per month, access to all public avatars, no watermarks on downloads, 3 script retries per video, 3 voice regenerations per video, and 5 link-to-video conversions per month.
    - **Ultra** - \$200/month. Includes 600 Velo minutes per month, 20 seats, up to 30 minutes of avatar video per month, your own custom VeloTwins, 10 script retries per video, 10 voice regenerations per video, unlimited link-to-video, and SSO/SAML support.

    See the [Pricing](/pricing/pricing) page for full details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are Velo Minutes?">
    Velo Minutes are the unit of usage on Velo. Each plan includes a monthly allowance that is shared across all workspace members. Minutes are consumed based on the length of the recordings and videos you generate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes. The Free plan gives you 15 Velo minutes per month, 3 seats, full editor access, and 1 video with avatar per month - no credit card required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
