Velo offers three plans. All plans give you access to the full editor, shared workspaces, and let you share videos without watermarks. You can start for free — no credit card required.

## Plans

### Free - \$0/month

Best for exploring Velo.

- **15 Velo minutes** per month (shared pool)
- **3 seats** per workspace
- Full access to the editor
- Share videos without watermarks
- 1 video with avatar per month
- Downloads are watermarked by default
- Remove watermark from a download anytime for **\$9.99**
- Top-up available: 15 minutes for \$5.99

---

### Pro - \$49/month

Best value for most users.

Everything in Free, plus:

- **60 Velo minutes** per month (shared pool)
- **5 seats** per workspace
- Up to **3 minutes** of avatar video per month
- Access to all public avatars
- No watermarks on downloads
- **3 script retries** per video
- **3 voice regenerations** per video
- **5 link-to-video** conversions per month

---

### Ultra - \$200/month

Built for high-volume output.

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **600 Velo minutes** per month (shared pool)
- **20 seats** per workspace
- Up to **30 minutes** of avatar video per month
- Your own custom VeloTwins
- **10 script retries** per video
- **10 voice regenerations** per video
- **Unlimited link-to-video** conversions
- SSO / SAML support

---

## What Are Velo Minutes?

Velo Minutes are the unit of usage across all plans. Each plan includes a monthly allowance that is shared across all workspace members. Minutes are consumed based on the length of the recordings and videos you generate each month. Your allowance resets at the start of every billing cycle.

## Seats

Each plan includes a set number of seats that determine how many people can belong to a workspace. If you need more seats, upgrade to a higher plan.

| Plan | Seats |
| --- | --- |
| Free | 3 |
| Pro | 5 |
| Ultra | 20 |

## Top-up

On the Free plan, you can purchase additional minutes if you run out before your cycle resets. Top-up packs are available from **Settings → Plans & Billing → Top-up**.

## Yearly Billing

All plans are available on a yearly billing cycle at a discounted rate. Switch between monthly and yearly on the [pricing page](https://usevelo.ai/pricing/).

## Questions?

If you have questions about which plan is right for you, reach out via the chat widget in the app or join the [Velo Community on Slack](https://velo-community.tightknit.community/).
