title: Create a Workspace
description: Set up a new workspace for your team in a few clicks.
---

Every Velo account starts with one workspace. You can create additional workspaces at any time — for example, one per team, department, or client.

## Creating a new workspace

**Step 1:** Click the workspace name at the top of the sidebar to open the workspace dropdown.

**Step 2:** Select **Create Workspace**.

**Step 3:** In the dialog that appears, enter a **Workspace name** and optionally upload a **Workspace logo** (JPG or PNG, max 2 MB).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/workspaces/create-workspace.jpg" alt="Create workspace dialog" />
</Frame>

**Step 4:** Click **Create a Workspace**. You'll be switched to the new workspace immediately.

## After creating your workspace

Your new workspace starts on the Free plan with 3 seats and 15 shared minutes per month. From here you can:

- [Invite members](/workspaces/invite-members) to start collaborating
- [Set up your Brand Kit](/workspaces/brand-kit) so every video matches your branding
- [Upgrade your plan](/settings/plans-billing) if you need more seats or minutes

<Note>
  You can rename your workspace or update its logo at any time from **Settings → General**.
</Note>
