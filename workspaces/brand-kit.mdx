The Brand Kit lets you define your workspace's visual identity. Once configured, your logo, brand color, and CTA button are automatically applied to every new video — keeping all team output on-brand without any manual setup from individual members.

<Note>
  Only Owners and Admins can configure the Brand Kit and CTA button.
</Note>

## Setting up your Brand Kit

Navigate to **Settings → Brand Kit**.

<Frame>
  ![Brand Kit settings page](/images/settings/brand-kit.jpg)
</Frame>

### Auto-match from your website

**Step 1:** Enter your company's website URL in the field at the top of the page.

Velo will pull your logo and brand color from the website automatically.

### Manual setup

If you don't have a website or prefer to set things manually, check **I don't have a website** and configure the following:

**Brand Logo** — Click **Upload brand logo** to upload your logo file. Square images (256 × 256 px) work best.

**Brand Color** — Enter a hex code or use the color picker to select your brand color.

**Step 2:** Click **Apply Brand Kit** to save.

## How it works

Once your Brand Kit is saved, it is automatically injected into every new chat session for all workspace members. Videos will include your logo and brand color by default.

Individual members can override the brand kit on a per-video basis in the editor if needed for a specific project.
