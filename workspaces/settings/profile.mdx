Your profile settings apply to your personal account across all workspaces. Navigate to **Settings → Profile** to manage them.

You can also get here by clicking the workspace dropdown and selecting **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/profile.jpg" alt="Profile settings page" />
</Frame>

## Profile info

Update your first name, last name, and profile picture. Your registered email is shown but cannot be changed from this page.

Click **Save changes** after making edits.

## Security

Click **Reset Password** to change your account password. You'll receive an email with a reset link.

## Devices

View all devices where your account is currently signed in. Each entry shows the browser, operating system, IP address, and last active time.

- **Log out** — end a single device session
- **Log out of all devices** — end every session, including the one you're currently using

## Danger Zone

Click **Delete account** to permanently remove your account and lose access to all workspaces. This action cannot be undone.
