Plans and billing are managed at the workspace level. Navigate to **Settings → Plans & Billing** to view your current plan and manage billing.

## Plans tab

The **Plans** tab shows your current plan and the upgrade options available. Each plan includes a set number of Velo minutes, seats, and features. See the [Pricing](/pricing/pricing) page for a full comparison.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/plans.jpg" alt="Plans & Billing page showing plan options" />
</Frame>

Click **Upgrade to Pro** or **Upgrade to Ultra** to switch plans.

## Top-up tab

If you're on the Free plan and run out of minutes before your cycle resets, you can purchase additional minutes from the **Top-up** tab.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/topup.jpg" alt="Top-up tab showing 15 minutes for $5.99" />
</Frame>

- **15 minutes** for **$5.99** ($0.40/min)

Click **Top up account** to complete the purchase. Top-up minutes are added to your workspace's shared pool immediately.

<Note>
  Top-up is available on the Free plan only. Pro and Ultra plans include higher minute allowances — consider upgrading if you regularly need more.
</Note>

## Invoices tab

View and download past invoices for your workspace.

## Payment methods tab

Add, update, or remove payment methods used for plan subscriptions and top-ups.
