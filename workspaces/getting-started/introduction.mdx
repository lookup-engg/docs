Welcome to Velo Docs. Velo is an AI-native video messaging tool that lets you create polished video messages — without turning on your camera or manually editing a single frame.

With Velo for Teams, your entire team works in a shared [workspace](/workspaces/overview). Videos, libraries, brand settings, and minutes are all managed together so everyone stays on the same page.

## Choose how to create your Velo

There are four ways to get started. Pick the one that fits your content:
