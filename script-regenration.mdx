### **Editing Your Scripts**

We want to make sure your AI voice matches the on-screen movement perfectly.

Small Changes: You can freely edit your script in the editor. However, to keep everything in sync, we limit manual additions to about 20% more words than the original draft.

### **Making Script Changes**

- Quick Edits: You can fix typos or swap out a few words directly in the editor.
- Major Rewrites: If your changes exceed 20% of the original character count, the editor will prompt you to redo the Assisted Recording. This ensures the AI can accurately map your new script to the product's flow.

### **How Retries Work**

If you aren't happy with the first draft, you can use a Retry to start fresh. We include a set of free retries in our paid plans so you can get the perfect take:

- Pro Plan: Includes 3 free retries per project.
- Ultra Plan: Includes 5 free retries per project.
- Beyond the Limit: Once you've used your free retries, each additional one costs 100 credits (equivalent to 1 minute of video) to cover the extra AI processing power.
