When you upload a PDF or provide a URL to generate a Velo, the system organizes your content into distinct scenes. Reviewing these scenes allows you to verify the visual pacing and spoken narration before the final video is produced.

## Review Scene Details

Scroll through the script cards to see the breakdown of your project. Each card represents a scene and contains two primary components: **Visual** describes the imagery or text that will appear on screen, and **Narration** contains the exact script that will be spoken by the selected voice. Each card also displays a timestamp range to indicate the duration of that specific segment.

## Adjust the Content

If the generated script does not meet your requirements, you can rewrite it entirely rather than editing individual cards.

**Step 1:** Navigate to the bottom of the script list.

**Step 2:** Select **Regenerate Script**.

**Step 3:** Provide specific feedback in the prompt box to guide the AI on the required changes — such as adjusting the tone or tightening the dialogue.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/ai-script/flow-b/review/scene-5.png" alt="Regenerate Script button at the bottom of the editor" />
</Frame>
Use the Regenerate Script option to rewrite the entire draft based on your feedback.

Once you are satisfied with the visual and narration sections, select **Go, Make my Velo** to proceed with video creation.
