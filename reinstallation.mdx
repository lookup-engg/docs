\
**Fix issues by uninstalling and reinstalling the Velo Chrome extension.**\
If you’re running into issues with the Velo Chrome extension, reinstalling it can often fix the problem. This guide will walk you through uninstalling and reinstalling the extension.

1. **Remove the extension:**

   Open your extensions page by visiting chrome://extensions in your browser. Find Velo in the list and click Remove.
2. **Quit and restart your browser:**

   After removing the extension, fully quit and restart Chrome. If you’re using a Mac, you can do this by holding the ‘command’ key and pressing ‘Q’. On Windows, close all Chrome windows from the taskbar or system tray.

   Note: Do not just close and reopen the browser tab/window. Make sure to quit the browser completely.
3. **Reinstall Velo:**

   Once Chrome restarts, reinstall the extension from the Chrome Web Store: Velo Chrome Extension
4. **Pin and sign in:**

   After reinstalling, pin the Velo extension for quick access. Open it and sign back in with your Velo account. You should now be good to go.
