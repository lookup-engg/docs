### **Can I add automatic zooms after my Velo is made?**

**No**, Velo does not automatically generate new zoom points once the video has been synthesised. However, you can control the behavior of existing zooms using **Zoom mode**. In \
**Auto** mode, the camera will automatically move to maintain visibility of your mouse cursor as it navigates the page.

### **Can I change the zoom levels in my Velo?**

**Yes.** Within the Zoom editor, use the **Zoom Level** slider to adjust how closely the camera focuses on your screen.

- You can set a specific zoom percentage (e.g., **50%**) to ensure clear visibility.
- If you want to return to the standard view, simply click the **Reset** button next to the slider.

### **Can I add manual zooms on my Velo?**

**Yes.** By switching the **Zoom mode** to **Manual**, you gain the ability to lock the camera onto a specific area of your video.

- This allows you to highlight a specific UI element or section of the page, even if the cursor moves elsewhere.
- You can select the exact portion of the frame you want to zoom into directly on the preview window.

### **Can I change the zoom effect in my Velo?**

**Yes.** You have several options to customize how transitions appear:

- **Instant Animation:** Toggle this switch to make the camera jump immediately to the zoomed-in view rather than using a smooth transition.
- **Disable Zoom:** If you want to keep your zoom settings but prevent them from appearing in the final video, enable the **Disable zoom** toggle. The zoom layer will remain saved, but the camera will stay at a standard distance.
