### **1. Open Microphone Settings**

In Google Chrome, copy and paste this link into your address bar: chrome://settings/content/microphone.

If you are using Arc, use this link instead: arc://settings/content/microphone.

### **2. Select Your Preferred Microphone**

From the dropdown menu, choose the microphone you want to use. In most cases, you can leave this as the default setting; you can also switch microphones directly within the Velo interface before you start recording.

### **3. Grant Access to Velo**

Scroll down to find the list of sites allowed to use your microphone.

- Look for [**usevelo.ai**](http://usevelo.ai) or the **Velo extension** under the **Allowed** section.
- If Velo is listed under **Not allowed**, click the arrow next to it and change the setting to **Allow**.

### **4. Quick Access via Browser Prompt**

When you start a session in Velo, your browser will show a prompt asking for microphone access. For the best experience, click **Allow on every visit**. This ensures you can move at the speed of thought without being interrupted by permission pop-ups every time you record.
