---
title: "Generating Speech"
---

In Velo, your script doesn't just provide the text, it drives the entire performance. Anytime you refine your narration or adjust the pacing, you’ll need to regenerate the script to ensure your **Digital Twin** and the browser agent are in perfect sync.

Here is how to generate and update your project script.

### **Updating Your Video Narration**

Velo is designed for a "one-shot" perfect take. When you modify your text, the system must re-map those words to your voice clone and the browser's actions.

- **Generate for the Entire Project**: This is the best choice when you have made multiple changes across different scenes. It ensures the entire walkthrough flows naturally and that every visual action matches the new audio.
- **Scene-Specific Updates**: If you have only adjusted the wording in one small section, you can update just that specific Scene Card. This is a fast way to fix a single sentence without re-processing the rest of the project.
