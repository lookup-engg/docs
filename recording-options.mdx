---
title: "Recording Options"
---

### **1. Paste Link**

Choose the **Product URL** option when your walkthrough takes place on a specific web application or website.

- **How it works**: You paste the link, and Velo’s agent navigates the site, clicks buttons, and scrolls based on your script.
- **When to use**: This is the best choice for high-quality product demos because it allows the agent to map the site directly for a perfect take.

### **2. Upload File (PDF or Slides)**

Choose **Import File** if you want to create a video based on a static presentation or document.

- **How it works**: You upload a PDF or image-based deck, and Velo uses these as the visual scenes for your video.
- **When to use**: This is ideal for quarterly reviews, pitch decks, or onboarding guides that don't require live website interaction.
