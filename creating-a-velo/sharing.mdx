Once your Velo is generated, you can share it with others via a public link or export the assets — including the video file and generated documentation — for offline use.

## Publishing Your Video

Before a shareable link is available, the video must be rendered.

**Step 1:** Click the **Share** button in the top right corner of the interface.

**Step 2:** In the **Share Velo** modal, click **Publish**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 26 At 9 59 34 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-26-at-9.59.34-PM.png)
</Frame>

Initiate the rendering process by clicking Publish.

The rendering process typically takes a few minutes, depending on the size and complexity of your project. You can track the progress through the status bar in the sharing modal.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 26 At 10 01 14 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-26-at-10.01.14-PM.png)
</Frame>

\
The modal displays the real-time rendering percentage.

## Distributing with a Share Link

As soon as rendering completes, a unique URL is generated. Copy this link to share your Velo with anyone — they can watch the video directly in their browser.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 26 At 10 01 58 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-26-at-10.01.58-PM.png)
</Frame>

The completed modal provides a shareable URL and download buttons.

## Exporting Documents and Video Files

In addition to the video, Velo generates companion documentation you can download in multiple formats. Use the **PDF** or **Markdown** buttons to save the written companion to your project, or use the **Download MP4** button to save the video file to your device.

On the free plan, downloaded videos will include a watermark. This watermark is removed for users on paid plans.
