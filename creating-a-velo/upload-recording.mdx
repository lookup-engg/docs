---
title: "Upload a Recording"
description: "Turn an existing screen recording or document into a polished Velo."
---

The AI agent analyses your uploaded file, extracting screen activity, narration audio, or document content and uses it to build a fresh, clean video. It applies optimised pacing, smart zooms, and AI narration to the output automatically.

Supported formats: MP4, MOV, and PDF.

## Step 1: Upload your file

From the dashboard, click **Upload a recording or PDF**. Select your file from your computer. Once uploaded, the AI agent begins analysing the content.

## Step 2: Wait for analysis

Choose a file from your browser. Once the file is opened, it is added to the platform for analysis.

<Frame>
  ![Velo dashboard upload options](/images/creating-a-velo/upload-recording/scene-4.png)
</Frame>

Choose the upload option to start a new project from an existing file.

## Step 3: Select your voice

Once analysis is complete, choose the voice that will narrate your video.

- **My Voices** - uses your personal VeloTwin voice clone
- **Public Voices** - uses a standard AI narrator from Velo's voice library

<Tip>
  If you haven't set up a Voice Clone yet, select a Public Voice to continue. You can set up your VeloTwin later and rebuild the video with your own voice.
</Tip>

<Frame>
  ![Voice selection interface](/images/creating-a-velo/upload-recording/scene-11.png)
</Frame>

Select a cloned or public voice for the video narration.

## Step 4: Confirm and generate

Confirm your selection to begin video generation. The agent will prompt you to proceed - click **"Yes, make my Velo"** to start.

A progress bar shows the status as the agent builds the final video. You can safely navigate away from the page - Velo will send you an email notification when your video is ready.

<Frame>
  ![Video generation progress bar](/images/creating-a-velo/upload-recording/scene-12.png)
</Frame>

Track the status of your video as the AI agent applies final edits.

The finished video features your uploaded content with cleaned-up pacing, synchronised AI narration, and automatic zoom events applied to key interactions.
