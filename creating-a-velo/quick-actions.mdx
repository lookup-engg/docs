Once a video is generated, the Velo chat panel provides a suite of quick actions to refine the output without needing to enter the full timeline editor. These tools allow for rapid adjustments to visuals, audio, and the underlying script.

## Customizing Visuals and Audio

**Step 1:** Access the **Quick actions** menu in the chat panel to see available modifications, including **Disable Zooms**, **Add BGM**, and **Add Logo**.

<Frame>
  ![Quick actions menu](/images/creating-a-velo/quick-actions/scene-1.png)
</Frame>

The Quick actions panel offers one-click shortcuts for common video edits.

**Step 2:** Select "**Add Music"** to open the music library. Choose from tracks like **Momentum**, **Fresh Start**, or **Subtle Touch** to change the mood of the video.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 26 At 9 50 29 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-26-at-9.50.29-PM.png)
</Frame>

\
The background music menu allows you to preview and apply different audio tracks.

**Step 3:** Update the visual style by choosing a **Background**. Select from **macOS** inspired styles, **Abstract** gradients, or click **Uploaded** to provide your own image.

## Refining Script and Voice

**Step 1:** Use **Change Voice** to swap the narrator or **Rewrite script** to modify the spoken content.

**Step 2:** When rewriting, select a persona - such as **Product Manager**, **Founder**, or **Marketing Strategist**  and define the target audience (e.g., **Executives**) to ensure the tone matches your goals.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 27 At 8 57 04 PM](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-27-at-8.57.04-PM.png)
</Frame>

Rewriting the script allows for persona and audience-based tone adjustments.

**Step 3:** Click **Republish video** to apply all changes and generate a new shareable link.

## Exporting and Advanced Editing

**Step 1:** If the quick actions are insufficient, select **Open full editor** to access exhaustive editing tools and more detailed background options.

**Step 2:** Once satisfied with the results, select **Download MP4** to save the final video file locally.

These quick actions provide a streamlined way to iterate on your content directly through the AI assistant interface before finalizing your project.
