Velo provides you 2 options to select what you want to record. You can
either provide us with a link to your product or a file.

![Description](/images/assisted-recording/recording-options/1.png)

### **1. Paste Link**

Choose the Product URL option when your walkthrough takes place on a
specific web application or website.

**How it works**: You paste the link, and Velo's agent navigates the
site, clicks buttons, and scrolls based on your script.

**When to use**: This is the best choice for high-quality product demos
because it allows the agent to map the site directly for a perfect take.

OR

### **2. Upload File (PDF or Slides)**

Choose Import File if you want to create a video based on a static
presentation or document.

**How it works**: You upload a PDF or image-based deck, and Velo uses
these as the visual scenes for your video.

**When to use**: This is ideal for quarterly reviews, pitch decks, or
onboarding guides that don\'t require live website interaction.
