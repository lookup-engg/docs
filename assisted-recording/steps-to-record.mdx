**Assisted Recording** is the collaborative part of the recording a Velo
process. Instead of following a strict script, you simply explain your
product flow as you click through it, and the tool captures your intent
to create a clean video later.

Let's learn how to record your session:

### **1. Start the Session**

Once you have your URL or file open, click the Start Session button at
the bottom of your screen to begin.

![Description](/images/assisted-recording/steps-to-record/1.png)

### **2. Guide the Tool**

Explain what is happening on screen in your own words as you move
through the product.

![Description](/images/assisted-recording/steps-to-record/2.png)

### **3. Fix Mistakes on the Fly**

If you stumble or want to re-explain a step, click Restart to clear your
current audio and try the complete assisted recording again. (Note:
Using Restart will not allow you to re-record just one section.)

![Description](/images/assisted-recording/steps-to-record/3.png)

### **4. Finish and Process**

Click Finish Session when you're done with assisted recording to let the
tool generate your draft script.

![Description](/images/assisted-recording/steps-to-record/4.png)
