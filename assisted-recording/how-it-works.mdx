Assisted recording, or "record my intent," allows you to guide the AI through a document or website by narrating your specific requirements rather than typing a long prompt. This is particularly useful when you need to specify exactly which sections to emphasize, skip, or explain in detail.

## Initializing the Recording

**Step 1:** Select the option to **Record my screen intent instead of typing a prompt** within the chat interface.

**Step 2:** Click **Start recording** to begin the setup process. This triggers the system to load your document or provided link into a workspace canvas.

## Setting Up the Session

**Step 1:** Once the document appears in the canvas, click **Start Session** to prepare the recording environment.

**Step 2:** If prompted, open the browser extensions menu and select the **Velo** extension to grant necessary permissions.

**Step 3:** Wait for the visual countdown to complete. You can click anywhere on the screen to pause the countdown if you need more time to prepare.

<Frame>
  ![On-screen countdown before recording starts](/images/assisted-recording/how-it-works/scene-7.png)
</Frame>

The countdown before the intent recording begins.

## Narrating Your Intent

**Step 1:** Speak clearly to explain what you want the AI to capture — for example, provide a brief overview of the first two pages and specify which subsequent pages should be skipped.

**Step 2:** Navigate through the document as you talk, ensuring your voiceover matches the sections you are referencing.

**Step 3:** When finished, click "**Finish Session"** to stop the recording.

<Frame>
  ![Document view during recording session](/images/assisted-recording/how-it-works/scene-8.png)
</Frame>

Narrating intent while navigating the document.

## Processing the Walkthrough

After finishing the session, a modal will appear indicating **Uploading recording...** — do not close the tab during this time. Once the upload is complete, the chat will display **Processing your walkthrough...**.

<Frame>
  ![Chat interface showing processing status](/images/assisted-recording/how-it-works/scene-10.png)
</Frame>

The system processing the submitted walkthrough.

The AI will then use your recorded intent and visual guidance to generate the final output.
