### **Can I add background music to my Velo?**

Yes, you can add background music by enabling the **Background Music** toggle at the top of the menu. Velo provides a library of tracks, which you can preview and select to set the right mood for your walkthrough.

### **Can I upload background music to my Velo?**

No, the current version of the Velo editor does not support custom music uploads. You can choose from the pre-selected library available in the **Audio** tab.

### **Can I change the background music volume?**

Yes. Once background music is enabled, use the **Background Music Volume** slider to find the perfect balance. This allows the music to play softly in the background without overpowering your voice.

### **Can I change the master volume of my Velo?**

Yes. The **Master Volume** slider allows you to increase or decrease the overall sound level of your entire project. This affects everything from your voice clone to the background music simultaneously.

### **Can I change my narration volume after making a Velo?**

Yes. You can adjust your voice clone's loudness specifically using the **Narration Volume** slider. This is helpful if you want your voice to be more prominent over the system audio or music.

### **Can I mute my system audio after my Velo is made?**

Yes. If your screen recording captured unwanted sounds from your computer, you can enable the **Mute System Audio** toggle. Alternatively, you can use the **System Audio** slider to lower the volume of those specific sounds without muting them completely.
