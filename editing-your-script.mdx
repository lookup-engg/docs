### **How to Edit Your Script**

1. **Select a Scene Card**: Navigate through your project using the **Go to Next Step** buttons to find the specific part you want to change.
2. **Click Edit Script**: This opens the text box for the narration section of that scene.
3. **Refine Your Text**: Type your changes directly. You can fix mistakes, simplify sentences, or add new information.
4. **Add Tone Cues**: If you want a specific part to sound more professional or enthusiastic, add a cue in brackets at the start of the sentence.
5. **Finalize**: Once you are happy with all your scenes, click the blue **Go, Make my Velo** button to generate your final, automated take.
