---
title: "How Billing Works"
---

When you click on make a velo, you might see "Minutes Reserved" in your balance.

- Why are they reserved? When you click "Make my Velo," we set aside enough credits to cover the estimated length of your script. 
- What if the video is shorter? If the final video ends up being shorter than our estimate, we instantly put the extra credits back into your balance.
- What if it fails? If a video fails to render for any reason, you get a 100% refund of those reserved credits immediately. You never pay for a video you can't use.
