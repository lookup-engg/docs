Paste a Link lets you create a Velo demo straight from a live website or web app. You give Velo a URL, walk through the page with your microphone on, and then an AI agent goes back and records the full demo from that same URL using your approved script.

<Note>
  This method is currently in **Beta**. Some features may change as we improve the experience.
</Note>

## When to use Paste a Link

This method works best when:

- Your product is a **live web app or public URL** that the agent can access
- You want to demo a specific page, feature, or flow at a real URL
- You don't have a screen recording or static files ready

It's ideal for product demos, landing page walkthroughs, and feature showcases where the live product itself is the best visual.

<Note>
  Some websites use bot-detection or security services (like Cloudflare) that may block the agent from loading the page. If you run into this, try a different URL or use the **Capture Screen Recording** or **Upload a Recording** methods instead.
</Note>

## Requirements

- **A Velo account** — sign in at [app.usevelo.ai](https://app.usevelo.ai)
- **The Velo Chrome Extension** — required for the narration session. See [Chrome Extension Installation](/chrome-extension/installation) if you haven't installed it yet.
- **A publicly accessible URL** — the page must be reachable by the agent without authentication

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste your URL">
    Name your project and enter the URL of the page you want to demo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Narrate the page">
    Velo opens the URL in a new tab. Walk through the product with your microphone on, narrating what you'd want to highlight.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your VeloTwin">
    Choose your AI presenter, add instructions, set the style and target duration, and click Generate Script.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the script">
    Review the AI-generated script scene by scene. Edit narration, give feedback, and regenerate until it's right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the agent">
    Click "Go, Make my Velo →" and the AI agent browses your URL, recording the demo from your approved script.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and share">
    Open the Velo editor to refine, then share via link or download.
  </Step>
</Steps>
