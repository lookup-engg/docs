## Requirements

Before you start, make sure you have:

- **A Velo account** — sign in at [app.usevelo.ai](https://app.usevelo.ai)
- **The Velo Chrome Extension** — required for the narration session. See [Chrome Extension Installation](/chrome-extension/installation) if you haven't installed it yet.
- **A URL ready** — the page you want to demo. It must be publicly accessible without a login.

<Note>
  This method is currently in **Beta**. Some features may change as we improve the experience.
</Note>

## Choosing a good URL

The agent will navigate to and record from the URL you provide. For the best results:

- Use a URL that **doesn't require authentication** — the agent can't log in on your behalf
- Use a **stable, consistently loading page** — avoid pages with heavy pop-ups, onboarding flows, or session-dependent content
- Use your **product's most representative page** — a dashboard, feature page, or landing page where the core value is visible
- Avoid pages with **aggressive bot protection** (like Cloudflare challenges) as these may prevent the agent from loading the page

<Tip>
  If your product requires login, consider using a staging environment with public access, or use the **Capture Screen Recording** method instead so you can record through your own authenticated session.
</Tip>

## Before you narrate

During the narration session, a new tab will open with your URL and your microphone will be live. Before you start, think through:

- What areas of the page you want to highlight
- What you want to skip or not draw attention to
- Which elements are worth zooming into
- What context you want to explain in your voiceover

Having a rough mental script beforehand leads to a cleaner transcript and a better AI-generated script.

## Starting from the dashboard

From your Velo dashboard, click **Create New Velo**. On the creation method screen, click the **Paste a Link** card to begin. A modal will appear asking you to name your project and enter your URL.
