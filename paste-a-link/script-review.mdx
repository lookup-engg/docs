---
title: "Script Review"
description: "Review and edit the AI-generated script scene by scene before the agent records your Velo."
---

After Velo generates the script, you land on the **Script Review** page. This is your chance to read through what the AI wrote and make edits before the agent goes and records the actual Velo. The script review happens **before** the agent runs - so take a moment to get it right.

## How the script is structured

The script is divided into **scenes**. Each scene corresponds to a section of your Velo and has two parts:

- **Visual** - describes what the agent will focus on or interact with on the page during that scene
- **Narration** - the voiceover text the VeloTwin will speak

## Navigating scenes

Use the **← →** arrow buttons to move between scenes. The current scene number and total count are shown (e.g., **SCENE 1**) along with a timestamp range (e.g., **00:00 - 00:08**) showing where in the demo that scene falls.

## Editing narration

The **Narration** text for each scene is directly editable - click in the text area to make changes. If you want to revert your edits and restore the AI's original text, click **Reset** next to the narration field.

## Giving feedback

Each scene has a **thumbs up / thumbs down** control. Use this to flag scenes that are on the right track or need improvement. This feedback helps guide Velo if you choose to regenerate the script.

## Regenerating the script

If the overall script needs a more substantial rework, click **Regenerate Script** at the bottom of the page. Velo will rewrite the script from your narration transcript. You can regenerate as many times as needed.

<Note>
  The Visual direction for each scene is set by the agent and is not directly editable. If the agent is focusing on the wrong part of the page, try adjusting your **Additional Instructions** in VeloTwin Setup before regenerating.
</Note>

## Running the agent

Once the script looks right, check the **estimated cost** at the bottom of the page - this shows how many Velo Minutes your Velo will use and your current balance. When you're ready, click **Go, Make my Velo →** to launch the agent.
