## Microphone not working during narration

**Symptom:** The session starts but Velo can't hear your microphone, or the transcript comes back blank.

**Fix:** Check that your browser has microphone permission for Velo. In Chrome, click the lock icon in the address bar → Site settings → Microphone → Allow. Refresh and try again. Also make sure no other app is blocking the mic at the system level.

---

## The URL tab doesn't open

**Symptom:** After clicking Continue on the Assisted Recording modal, no new tab opens with your URL.

**Fix:** Check that the Velo Chrome Extension is installed and enabled. The extension is required to open the URL tab for recording. See [Chrome Extension Installation](/chrome-extension/installation). If the extension is installed, try refreshing the Velo page and starting again.

---

## Agent can't load the page (bot protection / Cloudflare)

**Symptom:** In the Browser Agent live feed, the page shows a Cloudflare or "Performing security verification" screen. The agent keeps waiting but can't get through.

**Fix:** The website you're trying to demo has bot-detection that's blocking the agent. This is common on sites like Product Hunt, some SaaS marketing pages, and other high-traffic sites. Unfortunately the agent cannot bypass these checks.

**Alternatives:**
- Use a staging URL or a different URL that doesn't have bot protection
- Use **Capture Screen Recording** instead, where you record through your own authenticated browser session
- Contact the Velo team if this is a recurring issue for your product

---

## Transcript is inaccurate or missing

**Symptom:** After finishing the narration, the transcript is empty, garbled, or doesn't match what you said.

**Fix:** This usually happens due to low microphone volume or background noise. Try narrating again in a quiet environment and speak clearly, closer to your microphone. Check your system mic settings to ensure the input volume is adequate.

---

## Script quality is poor

**Symptom:** The generated script is repetitive, off-topic, or doesn't reflect the product well.

**Fix:** Click **Regenerate Script** on the Script Review page. Before regenerating, update the **Additional Instructions** field in VeloTwin Setup with more specific guidance — describing your audience, tone, and what to focus on typically leads to much better results.

---

## Agent recording failed or got stuck

**Symptom:** The Agent Activity log stops updating, or you get an error during the recording.

**Fix:** Click **Cancel Session** and then click **Go make my Velo** to restart the agent from the Script Review page. If the agent repeatedly fails on the same URL, try a different URL or switch to **Capture Screen Recording**.
