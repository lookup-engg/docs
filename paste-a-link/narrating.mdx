---
title: "Narrating Your Walkthrough"
description: "Walk through your live product with your microphone to create a narration transcript."
---

After entering your project name and URL and clicking **Continue**, Velo shows the **Assisted Recording** modal. This explains what you're about to do — a quick narration pass where you walk through the live page while talking through it with your microphone on.

## The Assisted Recording modal

The modal shows your URL in a badge at the top, confirming which page the session will open. It outlines three steps:

1. **Allow your microphone** — grant mic access when your browser prompts you
2. **Walk through your product and talk** — navigate the live page and narrate as you go
3. **Hit finish — Velo takes over** — when you're done, return to the Velo tab and click Finished Recording

The modal also shows four hint cards to guide your narration:

- **What to click and show** — navigate to the parts of the product that matter most
- **What to skip** — avoid areas that aren't relevant to the demo
- **What to zoom into** — note any UI details worth highlighting closely
- **What to explain in voiceover** — think about what context needs verbal explanation

When you're ready, click **Continue** to start the session.

## The live session

Clicking Continue opens your URL in a **new browser tab** and starts recording your microphone. The main Velo tab shows:

> *"Session is live. Switch tabs to perform your walkthrough."*

Switch to the newly opened tab and begin walking through your product. Navigate the page naturally — click around, scroll, hover over elements — while narrating what you're showing. Speak as if you're explaining the product to a potential customer.

<Note>
  The session records your microphone from the moment you click Continue. Make sure your mic is enabled in your browser before starting.
</Note>

## Finishing the narration

When you've covered everything you want in the demo, switch back to the **main Velo tab** and click **Finished Recording**. The URL tab will close automatically.

Velo processes your audio into a transcript. You'll see a brief loading state while this completes. Once ready, you'll be taken to the **VeloTwin Setup** page.
