After your narration transcript is processed, Velo takes you to the **VeloTwin Setup** page. This is where you configure the AI agent - picking the presenter voice, setting the tone and style, and choosing how long the final Velo should be.

## Selecting a VeloTwin

The **Select Your VeloTwin** section shows your available AI presenters as a grid of cards. Each card shows a thumbnail and the presenter's name with their accent (for example, "Drew - American" or "Viraj - Indian").

Click the play icon on any card to hear a voice preview. Click a card to select that presenter.

Once selected, the grid collapses into a summary card showing your chosen VeloTwin, with a **Change VeloTwin** link if you want to switch.

<Warning>
  Your VeloTwin **cannot be changed after Velo generation**. Choose carefully - this selection is locked once you click "Go, Make my Velo."
</Warning>

### Public VeloTwins vs. My VeloTwins

The **Public VeloTwins** tab shows Velo's built-in library of AI presenters. The **My VeloTwins** tab shows any custom VeloTwins you've created. Learn how to create one in the [Creating VeloTwin](/creating-velotwin/overview) section.

## Additional instructions

The **Additional Instructions** field lets you give the agent specific guidance on how to write the script. Use it to shape the tone, audience focus, or messaging priorities. For example:

- *"Keep it conversational and jargon-free - this is for a non-technical audience."*
- *"Focus on the key workflow, not the settings or admin pages."*
- *"Highlight how this saves time for marketing teams."*

This field is optional, but adding a few clear sentences here meaningfully improves script quality.

## Default style

The **Default Style** dropdown sets the overall presentation style. Options include styles like **Default**, **Product Manager**, and others - choose the one that best fits how you want your Velo to feel.

## Duration

The **Duration** dropdown sets the target length for the generated Velo. Choose based on your intended use case - shorter for quick product teasers, longer for detailed walkthroughs.

## Generating the script

Once you've set everything up, click **Generate Script**. Velo's AI agent will analyse your narration transcript and the URL content to write a scene-by-scene script.

You'll be taken to the **Script Review** page when the script is ready.
