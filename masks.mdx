### **Can I hide my sensitive content on my generated Velo?**

**Yes**, you can hide sensitive information using the **Masks** feature. This tool allows you to cover or blur specific areas of your screen such as passwords, personal emails, or private dashboard data ensuring that your viewers only see the information you want to share.

### **Can I add 2 masks in the same video?**

**Yes.** The Velo timeline includes a dedicated **Masks** track where you can manage multiple entries. Similar to how you can have multiple **Zoom** blocks at different points in your video, you can add multiple mask segments to cover different sensitive areas throughout your walkthrough.
