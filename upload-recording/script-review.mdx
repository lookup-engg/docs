When your Velo finishes generating, you land in the editor. The **Scripts** panel on the right side contains the AI-generated narration script — Velo has analysed your recording and written a voiceover that describes what's happening on screen.

This is the right moment to review and refine the script before generating the voice.

## Reading the script

Click **Scripts** in the right-hand panel to open it. You'll see the narration text Velo wrote based on your recording. Read through it to check:

- Does it accurately describe what you showed?
- Does it highlight the right features and benefits?
- Does the tone match your brand?

The script is fully editable — click anywhere in the text to make changes.

## Rewriting with AI

If the script needs more than small edits, click **AI Rewrite**. Velo will use AI to rework the narration. You can run this multiple times until you're happy with the result.

## Generating the voice

Once the script reads the way you want it, click **Generate Voice**. Velo will synthesize the narration using the VeloTwin voice you selected earlier and sync it to your video.

<Note>
  Voice generation consumes one of your available generations. The panel shows how many free generations you have remaining.
</Note>

After generating, play your Velo in the canvas to hear the narration in sync with the video. If you want to make further edits to the script, update the text and click **Generate Voice** again — each generation replaces the previous one.
