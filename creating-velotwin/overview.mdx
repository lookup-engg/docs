A VeloTwin is your personal AI presenter - an avatar that looks and sounds like you, used to narrate your Velos. Once created, your VeloTwin is saved to your account and available instantly for every Velo you make.

## Public VeloTwins vs. My VeloTwins

Velo includes a library of **Public VeloTwins** - a set of built-in AI presenters you can use right away without any setup. These are great for getting started quickly or for Velos where a generic presenter is fine.

**My VeloTwins** are custom presenters you create yourself. They use your own face and voice, making your Velos feel personal and on-brand. If you're building Velos for sales, onboarding, or customer communication, a custom VeloTwin that looks and sounds like you (or your team) creates a much stronger impression.

## What you'll create

Creating a VeloTwin is a one-time process with two parts:

- **Your face** - a single photo taken through Velo's camera capture. Velo generates a photorealistic avatar from the photo and automatically handles natural cues like blinking and nodding.
- **Your voice** - a short recording of you reading a training script. Velo clones your vocal tone, pace, and character to produce a natural-sounding voice for your Velos.

Once both are submitted and processed, your VeloTwin is ready to use across any project.

## Where to create your VeloTwin

Go to the **VeloTwin** section from the left sidebar, then click **Create VeloTwin** to start the setup process.
