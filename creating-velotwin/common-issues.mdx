## Photo was rejected or looks wrong

**Symptom:** The face capture step doesn't accept your photo, or the avatar preview looks distorted or unrealistic.

**Fix:** Retake the photo with the following in mind:
- Make sure your face is **centered and well-lit** — avoid shadows across your face or strong backlight
- Look **directly into the camera**, not off to the side
- Keep your **expression neutral** — avoid extreme smiles or squinting
- Make sure no other faces are visible in the frame
- Avoid wearing hats, sunglasses, or anything that obscures your face

---

## Voice recording failed quality assessment

**Symptom:** After clicking Finish Recording, Velo tells you the audio quality isn't usable and asks you to retake it. The system may show specific feedback.

**Fix:** Read the feedback shown on screen — it will indicate what went wrong (e.g. too much background noise, low volume, clipping). Common fixes:

- Move to a **quieter room** and close windows and doors
- Check your **microphone input level** in your system settings — it should be high enough to register clearly without peaking into the red
- Stay **close to the microphone** and maintain a consistent distance throughout
- Speak at a **natural, confident pace** — don't trail off at the end of sentences

---

## Microphone not detected

**Symptom:** The voice recording step shows no microphone input or the recording is completely silent.

**Fix:** Check that your browser has microphone permission for Velo. In Chrome, click the lock icon in the address bar → Site settings → Microphone → Allow. Refresh the page and try again. Also confirm your microphone is set as the default input device in your system audio settings.

---

## VeloTwin generation takes too long or seems stuck

**Symptom:** After submitting your photo and voice, the generation step runs for a very long time without completing.

**Fix:** This step typically takes 1–3 minutes. If it's been significantly longer, try refreshing the page. If your VeloTwin appears in your **My VeloTwins** list, it completed successfully. If not, try the creation process again.

---

## VeloTwin voice doesn't sound like me

**Symptom:** The generated voice clone sounds flat, robotic, or doesn't capture your natural tone.

**Fix:** This usually happens when the training recording was too monotone or had quality issues. Delete the VeloTwin and create a new one, this time:
- Read the training script with **natural expressiveness** — vary your pace and emphasis as you normally would
- Use a **better microphone** or record in a quieter environment
- Speak at your **normal presenting pace**, not slower than usual
