Let's understand how to create your VeloTwin. Setting up your digital
face and voice is a simple, one-time calibration process.

### **1. Capture Your Face**

Velo generates your avatar from a single photo.

- **Setup**

> Look directly into your camera and center your face in the frame.

- **Capture**

> Follow the 3-second countdown to take your photo.

- **Background Selection**

> You've option to keep the current background or change your
> background.

- **Finalize**

> Review the preview and click **Continue** to save your digital
> likeness. Velo will automatically handle natural non-verbal cues like
> blinking and nodding.

![Description](/images/creating-velotwin/steps-to-create/1.png)

### **2. Clone Your Voice**

Velo captures your natural tonality to create a clone of your audio.

- **Record**

> Read the training script naturally and expressively.

- **Environment**

> Use your primary microphone in a quiet space for the best quality.

- **Finish**

> Click **Finish Recording** once you are done to save your audio clone
>
> (After recording, the system will assess the input quality. If the
> audio is usable, you can proceed to the next step. If not, you will be
> prompted to retake the recording and the system will display specific
> feedback to help you be more careful next time.)

![Description](/images/creating-velotwin/steps-to-create/2.png)

### **3. Generating Your Twin**

Once your photo and voice are submitted, Velo begins the synthesis to
generate your VeloTwin.\
\
Your Twin is now saved to your account and can be used to narrate any
future project instantly.

![Description](/images/creating-velotwin/steps-to-create/3.png)
