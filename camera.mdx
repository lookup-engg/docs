### **How to change my camera positioning in my Velo**

- Use the **Camera Position** grid to snap your avatar to any of the nine standard positions on the screen.
- Simply click a circle on the grid to instantly move your camera to that corner, side, or the center of the frame.

### **How to change my camera video size**

- Use the **Camera Size** slider to scale your avatar up or down.
- Moving the slider to the right increases your visibility, while sliding it to the left makes the camera more discreet.

### **How to change roundness of my camera video**

- Adjust the **Roundness** slider to modify the corner radius of your camera frame.
- Sliding this to the right will gradually turn a square frame into a rounded one.

### **How to change shape of my camera video**

- In the **Shape** section, you can quickly toggle between three presets:
  - **Original**: Your default captured frame.
  - **Square**: A perfectly even four-sided frame.
  - **Round**: A circular "bubble" look.

### **Can I add zoom effects on my camera video?**

- Yes, Velo includes an intelligent **Camera Size During zoom** feature.
- By enabling the **Make Smaller during zoom** toggle, your camera will automatically shrink when the video zooms into your screen recording. This ensures your avatar never blocks the content being highlighted.

### **Can I customize the camera zoom effect in my Velo?**

- Yes, you can control exactly how much your camera shrinks during a zoom event.
- Use the **Size during Zoom** slider to set the specific scale for your avatar when a zoom is active. Click **Reset** at any time to return to the default zoom behavior.
