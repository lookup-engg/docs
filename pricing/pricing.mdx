Velo offers three plans. All plans give you access to the full editor
and let you share videos without watermarks. You can start for free —
no credit card required.

## Plans

### Free — $0/month

Best for exploring Velo.

- **15 Velo minutes** per month
- Full access to the editor
- Share videos without watermarks
- 1 video with avatar per month
- Downloads are watermarked by default
- Remove watermark from a download anytime for **$9.99**

---

### Pro — $20/month

Best value for most users.

Everything in Free, plus:

- **60 Velo minutes** per month
- Up to **3 minutes** of avatar video per video
- Access to all public avatars
- No watermarks on downloads
- **3 script retries** per video
- **3 voice regenerations** per video
- **5 link-to-video** conversions per month

---

### Ultra — $200/month

Built for high-volume output.

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **600 Velo minutes** per month
- Up to **30 minutes** of avatar video per video
- Your own custom VeloTwins
- **10 script retries** per video
- **10 voice regenerations** per video
- **Unlimited link-to-video** conversions

---

## What Are Velo Minutes?

Velo Minutes are the unit of usage across all plans. They are consumed
based on the length of the recordings and videos you generate each
month. Your allowance resets at the start of every billing cycle.

## Yearly Billing

All plans are available on a yearly billing cycle at a discounted rate.
Switch between monthly and yearly on the [pricing page](https://usevelo.ai/#pricing).
