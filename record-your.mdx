### 1. Start your session

From the Velo Home Screen, click the blue **Generate a Velo** button. Choose **Paste a Link** for web apps or **Import File** for PDFs and slide decks.

Once your source is ready, click **Start Session** in the control bar at the bottom of your screen. Velo will enter a standby mode, waiting for you to begin.

### **3. Guide Velo Naturally**

When you are ready, speak naturally and explain what you are doing on screen.

- **Talk to a colleague:** Explain the flow as if you were talking to a teammate.
- **No script needed:** We will regenerate the voice according to the narration. We don't remove anything in the final version, we create a new version.
- **Click and Scroll:** Perform the actions you want to show, like clicking a button or scrolling a list. Our tool captures these movements as a context for our browser agent.

### **4. Restart if You Stumble**

If you want to re-explain a specific part, simply click **Restart** in the control bar. This clears your current speaking session so you can try again without having to reset your entire project.

### **5. Finish and Process**

When you have completed your walkthrough. Before the script is generated, you can provide **Additional Instructions** to the tool and once everything looks good click **Finish Session**. Velo will take a few moments to process your recording. Once finished, you will see:\
\
**Choose Your VeloTwin**

Next, you will be prompted to select the digital face and voice for your video.

- **Personal Clone:** Use your own calibrated **VeloTwin** for a personal touch.
- **Public Library:** Choose a professional avatar if you haven't set up your own yet.

#### **Review Refined Scene Cards**

After processing, Velo presents your walkthrough as a series of **Scene Cards**.

- **Draft Script:** See the narration Velo has written based on your guided session.
- **Visual Actions:** Review the list of autonomous actions like clicks and scrolls that the browser agent will perform.
- **Final Edits:** You can modify the text or visual instructions here to make the video perfect.
