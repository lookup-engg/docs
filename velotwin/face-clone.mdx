---
title: "Face Clone"
description: "Create a custom digital face clone to maintain a consistent presence across all your Velo video projects."
---

Velo allows you to create a custom face clone to use across your video projects. This feature enables you to maintain a consistent digital presence without needing to record new footage for every message. You can choose to use public faces or build a personalized version of yourself.

## Accessing the Face Clone Gallery

**Step 1:** Select **Face clone** from the left-hand navigation panel.

**Step 2:** On the **Face Clone** dashboard, select the **Create face clone** button.

## Capturing Your Image

**Step 1:** Enter a name for your new clone in the **Name your face clone** field.

**Step 2:** Select **Click picture**.

**Step 3:** When prompted by your browser, allow camera access to initialize the capture interface.

**Step 4:** Align yourself within the frame and follow the on-screen countdown to take your photo.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/velotwin/face-clone/scene-4.png" alt="Naming the face clone" />
</Frame>
Naming the new face clone before capturing a photo.

## Customizing the Background

Once your picture is captured, the system provides several options to refine the look of your VeloTwin. You can browse through various environmental presets — such as an office setting — by selecting **Change Background**. If the initial capture isn't ideal, select **Retake** to try again. When satisfied with the preview, select **Continue** to save the clone.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/velotwin/face-clone/scene-8.png" alt="Background customization" />
</Frame>
Applying a custom background to the face clone capture.

## Using Your Face Clone

After saving, your custom clone appears in the **Custom Face Clone** section of your library. This clone is now ready to be selected and applied to any new video messages you create within Velo.
