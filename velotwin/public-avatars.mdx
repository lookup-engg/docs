Velo provides an integrated library of public actors, offering high-quality synthetic voices and faces that are ready for immediate use in video projects. These pre-built assets allow for professional-grade narration and presentation without requiring custom clones.

## Public Voice Actors

The platform features a library of six versatile public voices — three male and three female — covering a range of regional accents to suit diverse content requirements.

- **Drew** – American Male
- **Justin** – American Male
- **Viraj** – Indian Male
- **Hope** – American Female
- **Monika** – Indian Female
- **Chloe** – French Female

## Public Face Actors

Public face clones are designed to work in tandem with the voice library. Six available public faces correspond directly to the voices listed above, ensuring a natural and synchronized appearance when paired together.

A key distinction between actor types is their accessibility. While custom face clones require a Velo Ultra plan subscription, public face clones are available to all users — making it possible on any tier to produce videos featuring lifelike actors without a premium subscription.

## How Public Actors Work

Because the public faces are pre-mapped to the public voices, they provide a cohesive and professional presentation style. By selecting an actor from the library, you instantly apply a consistent persona to your video messages — without any recording equipment or a custom clone plan.
