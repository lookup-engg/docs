Velo allows you to customize and structure your generated documents through direct text editing and a command-driven formatting menu. You can modify the narrative, adjust the hierarchy of information, and insert visual evidence directly from your project's recorded frames.

## Modifying Content

To edit existing text, click anywhere on the page and begin typing. You can update descriptions or rewrite sections to better align with your specific requirements.

## Adding Sections and Formatting

To introduce new sections or change the style of your content, use the command menu.

**Step 1:** Create a new line and type a forward slash /.

**Step 2:** Select a formatting option from the dropdown menu. \
Options include; **Heading 1, Heading 2**, and **Heading 3** for document structure; **Bullet List** and **Numbered List** for sequential data; **Code** blocks for technical snippets; **Quote** for emphasis; and **Divider** to separate themes visually.to separate themes visually.

## Inserting Project Screenshots

You can supplement your documentation with visual context by pulling screenshots directly from the video generation process.

**Step 1:** Type `/` and select **Image** from the menu.

**Step 2:** Browse the **Insert screenshot** gallery, which contains all frames captured during the Velo's creation.

**Step 3:** Click a specific frame to embed it into your document.
